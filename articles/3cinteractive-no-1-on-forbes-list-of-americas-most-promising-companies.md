# 3CInteractive No. 1 on Forbes’ list of America’s Most Promising Companies

[**[![3Cilogo](https://web.archive.org/web/20150913050157im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017d4092f2fe970c-200wi "3Cilogo")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017d4092f2fe970c-pi)3Cinteractive**](http://www.3cinteractive.com/ "3Cinteractive"), a leading mobile platform
company with a strong focus on consumer engagement, announced
it ranked #1 on *Forbes’* list of “America’s Most Promising
Companies.” The third annual list recognizes U.S.-based, high-growth, privately
held companies. The full list of companies will be available online at [**www.forbes.com**](http://www.forbes.com/ "Forbes") Feb. 6.

“It is an honor to
rank at the top of *Forbes’* list of America’s Most Promising
Companies,” said **[John Duffy,
3Cinteractive’s founder and CEO](http://www.3cinteractive.com/About/Management/ "John Duffy, Founder and CEO")**of the Boca Raton-based technology company launched in 2005. “This recognition is a
testament to our amazing team and their tireless work ethic, as well as our
world-class clients and partners who make our success possible. We will continue to invest in our Switchblade platform to help our clients create stronger engagement with consumers on their mobile devices in ways that drive measurable, profitable results,” he said on 3Ci's blog.
