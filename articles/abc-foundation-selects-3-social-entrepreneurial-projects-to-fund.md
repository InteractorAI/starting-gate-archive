# abc* Foundation selects 3 social entrepreneurial projects to fund

The [Americas
Business Council (abc\*) Foundation](http://www.abcfound.org/), a nonprofit organization that
brings high-impact leaders and organizations together to confront pressing social and environmental issues in the Americas, has selected **Fairtrasa, Naya Jeevan**and**Planeta Océano** as its new partner organizations to support, following the [2012 abc\* Continuity Forum](http://www.abccontinuityforum.com/) where these organizations presented
alongside 29 other social entrepreneurs. These three
organizations will receive funding and two years of advisory, media and
marketing support. Here are
the three selected organizations:

**FAIRTRASA**helps small-scale farmers in third-world countries escape poverty
and improve their lives by providing them with technical support and access to
international markets.  The company helps to ensure that farmers are paid
fair prices, which can often be up to 10 times higher than
local market prices, and help them increase their yields. [www.fairtrasa.com](http://www.fairtrasa.com/)

**NAYA JEEVAN** is a nonprofit micro-insurance company
dedicated to rejuvenating the lives of the urban poor by providing them with
access to quality and affordable healthcare, insurance and socio-economic
opportunities.  The company buys group health plans from
insurance companies at below-market rates and leverages corporate distribution
networks to distribute, market and
co-finance the plans. [www.njfk.org](http://www.njfk.org/)

**PLANETA OCÉANO**is committed to conserving and restoring
coastal and marine environments—vital life support systems—that are threatened
by overexploitation, pollution, habitat degradation and climate change. 
Planeta Océano centers its efforts, which primarily occur in low-income areas
in Peru, on  research, environmental education and sustainable development of coastal communities. [www.planetaoceano.org](http://www.planetaoceano.org/)
