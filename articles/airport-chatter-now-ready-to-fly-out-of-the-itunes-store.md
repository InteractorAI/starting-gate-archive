# Airport Chatter now ready to fly out of iTunes store

[![Airports](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2017d3f01f54c970c-120wi.jpg "Airports")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017d3f01f54c970c-pi)
[![Airportchattersignup](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2017ee67d0018970d-120wi.jpg "Airportchattersignup")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017ee67d0018970d-pi)Izzy Kirsh of Miami
Beach and his three co-founders from Toronto, Sao Paulo and Sydney, Australia just launched
[Airport Chatte](http://www.airportchatter.com/)r, a free mobile app that provides information, tools, reviews
and interactions aimed at making air travel a better experience in 30 of the busiest
airports in the U.S., including MIA. Features include "Radar" to connect in person with other travelers and a real-time feed of airport experiences.

Soon to come, the team says: More features such as check-in and baggage tracking and expansion into the UK, Canada, Australia, Brazil, Germany, Singapore, Hong
Kong and Dubai. Airport Chatter is available on the iPhone, with Android
rollout soon to follow.
