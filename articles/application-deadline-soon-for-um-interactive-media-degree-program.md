# Application deadline soon for UM interactive media degree program

The [**University of Miami School of Communicatio**](http://www.com.miami.edu/)[**n**](http://com.miami.edu/) launched a new**[Interactive Media Program](http://interactive.miami.edu/)** offering a Master's of Fine Art  in Interactive Media. The program starts in fall 2013.  
  
The university said the  multidisciplinary curriculum will involve gaming, mobile, data visualization, interaction design, and other emerging technologies and the program trains students to research, prototype, design, and build projects in business, social, academic and cultural contexts. The curriculum includes classes such as Infographics and Visualization, Technology Trends, User Interface Design Development, Game Development and more.  
  
Applications for the M.F.A. in Interactive Media are due by May 1, 2013  for the fall semester. Click [here](http://interactive.miami.edu/mfa-in-interactive-media-application/) for application requirements.  [Apply here](http://interactive.miami.edu/mfa-in-interactive-media-application/). For more information, visit [http://interactive.miami.edu/](http://interactive.miami.edu/%20)or contact [**Prof. Kim Grinfeder**](http://com.miami.edu/profile/Grinfeder,Kim) at grinfeder@miami.edu.
