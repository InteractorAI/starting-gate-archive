# Apply now to present at Florida Venture Forum

The [Florida Venture Forum](http://www.flventure.org/) is calling for outstanding private Florida companies interested in applying to present at its 24th annual Florida Venture Capital Conference, to be held Jan. 28-29, 2014 at the Hyatt Regency Orlando (formerly known as the Peabody Orlando Hotel).    
  
In addition to keynote speakers, panel discussions, and networking opportunities, the 2014 Florida Venture Capital Conference will coincide with the Florida Venture Forum's 30th Anniversary, and many "Forum Alumni" will be on hand to share their stories of growth and success.    
  
Each year the Florida Venture Capital Conference showcases 20-25 later stage, high growth private companies before an audience of venture capitalists, private equity investors and other investor sources. Entrepreneurial companies should have talented management teams, proprietary technology, revenue, and high growth potential. Although presenters are typically seeking later stage funding, applicants are welcome from qualified companies even if they are not actively seeking funding.

Companies wishing to present must apply online and submit a **[PRESENTER APPLICATION](http://www.flventure.org/events/2014-florida-venture-capital-conference/presenters-application.aspx?utm_source=Apply+Now+To+Present+At+The+2014+Florida+Venture+Capital+Conference&utm_campaign=FVF+Campaign&utm_medium=email)** by the early deadline of **Friday, Nov. 15, 2013**.
