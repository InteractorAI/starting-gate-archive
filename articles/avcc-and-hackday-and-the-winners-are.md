# AVCC and HackDay: And the winners are...

[![Trapezoid N1eZwsW_uA9JEt6kT_Dwi6AZwWepd8egZOvbOwE5m_o](https://web.archive.org/web/20121215214032im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017d3ecf85f3970c-320wi "Trapezoid N1eZwsW_uA9JEt6kT_Dwi6AZwWepd8egZOvbOwE5m_o")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017d3ecf85f3970c-pi)Twenty-nine startup companies presented at the [Americas Venture Capital Conference](http://avcc.fiu.edu/), competing for investors’ time and money. These companies were also awarded prizes:

•  Tres Mares Innovation Prize ($10,000 cash and artist rendering valued at $5,000): Trapezoid Digital Security of Miami (pictured at left, with FIU Entrepreneur in Residence Boris Hirmas Said.)

•  CP Capital Later Stage Company ($15,000 in investment banking services): itMD of Miami

•  CP Capital Early Stage Company ($10,000 in services): Cognicor of Spain

[![FotopigeonoMVE-cQVgqf72hggGshKdA17je1Dbg02kMjIlozI6as](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2017d3ecf81af970c-320wi.jpg "FotopigeonoMVE-cQVgqf72hggGshKdA17je1Dbg02kMjIlozI6as")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017d3ecf81af970c-pi)•  New Frontier Nomads Idea Validation and Micro-MVP Prize ($10,000 in services): fotopigeon of Tampa (pictured at right in the "Hot Zone.")

•  Miami Innovation Fund Startup Prize ($20,000 term sheet): fotopigeon of Tampa

HackDay winners

Five startups, all finalists from [HackDay](http://www.hackday.co/), competed for a $30,000 cash prize plus an additional $20,000 if the team starts a company. And the winner is: AskMeOut from Brazil. Runner-up: Introduced.co of Miami won a year of free co-working space at LAB Miami.
