# AT&T Mobile App Hackathon: And the winners are...

[![Hackathon Photo 2](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2017c34b7af38970b-320wi.jpg "Hackathon Photo 2")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017c34b7af38970b-pi)Miami's second [AT&T Mobile App Hackathon](att-mobile-app-hackathon-is-coming-back-to-miami.md) concluded this weekend, with nine teams presenting at the RightSpace2Meet co-working center at the University of Miami Life Science & Technology Park.

About 100 turned out for the hackathon, and 65 competed, said Milana Kuznetsova of ESENEM, which produced the Hackathon with AT&T. Here are the winning teams, who split up prize packages worth about $15,000:

**Best Blackberry 10 App:** Ya Grub

**Best Blackberry 10 Cascades App:** Sphero Drive

**Best Overall:** 1st - Clutch Traveler

2nd - Spheromon

3rd - Likr

**Best AT&T API App:** Clutch Traveler

**Best Windows Azure App:** NowApp

**Best App for ESENEM:** SpyNotes

**Best Sphero App:** Spheromon

**Best Native Android App:** Spheromon

Congratulations to all the teams.
