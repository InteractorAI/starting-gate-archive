# Brian Brackeen named Innovator of the Year at TechConneXT

![BrianBrackeen](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d232b8de970c-800wi.jpg "BrianBrackeen")

Congratulations to Brian Backeen, [Kairos](http://florida-institute.us6.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=ab8dd9983cc108eb7c951b935&id=78c6790f7c&e=e9915bdcd3) founder and CEO who was recently named Innovator of the Year at [Black Enterprise TechConneXt Summit](http://florida-institute.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=ab8dd9983cc108eb7c951b935&id=8b1fdf2179&e=e9915bdcd3) for his notable contributions to the tech industry. He offered attendees candid insight on fundraising, navigating challenges, and ultimately the road to excelling in tech.

Kairos is a human analytics company that uses three-dimensional data to measure human feelings and interactions, and was recently featured in [Florida Trend](http://florida-institute.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=ab8dd9983cc108eb7c951b935&id=14786ae6a0&e=e9915bdcd3) as a promising new company. Brackeen is  an Endeavor entrepreneur, and Kairos has company, has received local funding from New World Angels, AGP and the Florida Institute for the Commercialization of Public Research as well as other Florida-based and national angel groups and VCs.
