# Can I buy you a drink from afar? Yep, with Airtab

[![Airtab](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201a3fd31fa7e970b-120wi.jpg "Airtab")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a3fd31fa7e970b-pi)Imagine it’s your best friend’s birthday but you are home with the flu. Airtab allows you to send your friend his/her favorite cocktail. Gift a drink to your co-worker who has joined you at happy hour or just as easily, gift a drink to someone across town or out of town.

Airtab is a mobile gifting app that gives consumers the ability to send drinks or buy a bottle with just the tap of a finger, says its founder, Dennis Cunningham, CEO of Perfect Vodka. It's available now in the [Apple® app store](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/airtab/id775158689?mt=8) and soon to be available in the Google Play store as well.

 All drinks and products can be redeemed at Airtab**-**friendly establishments with a full list available at [http://www.Airtab.me/about](http://www.airtab.me/about).  Current establishments include JJ Muggs Stadium Grill, The Black Rose and Dania Beach Grill in Palm Beach County and 94th Aero Squadron and 5 Napkin Grill in Miami-Dade. The app allows the user to choose from two tiers of drinks -- Premium and Standard.

 With every purchase made, the user can donate a portion of the tab to a participating charity of their choosing.  Charities include the Glaucoma Foundation, Good Karma Pet Rescue, Operation Home Front and Feeding South Florida. We'll drink to that.
