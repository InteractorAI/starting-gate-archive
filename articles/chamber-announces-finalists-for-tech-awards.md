# Greater Miami Chamber announces finalists for Technology Leader of Year awards

The [Greater Miami Chamber of Commerce](http://www.miamichamber.com/) has named announced the finalists for its [Technology Leaders of the Year](http://www.miamichamber.com/cwt/External/WCPages/WCEvents/EventDetail.aspx?EventID=3262) Awards. Winners will be announced at its April 11 luncheon event at Jungle Island. More information on the [event is here.](http://www.miamichamber.com/cwt/External/WCPages/WCEvents/EventDetail.aspx?EventID=3262)
