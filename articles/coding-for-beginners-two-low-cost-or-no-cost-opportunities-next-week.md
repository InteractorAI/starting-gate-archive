# Coding for beginners: Two low-cost or no-cost opportunities next week

**[![YPN Intro to Coding workshop](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2019b0045d74b970c-320wi.jpg "YPN Intro to Coding workshop")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019b0045d74b970c-pi)**Next week brings two opportunities for beginners -- adults and kids -- to be introduced to some aspects of coding.

**Crack the Code!**

 Want to develop your own mobile app or create your own website?

Learn what coding is all about at the FREE *Intro to Coding* workshop for beginners presented by the Miami-Dade Chamber of Commerce and the Young Professionals Network of Miami.

The details:

6 p.m. to 8:30 p.m., Wednesday, Oct. 30, Miami Dade College North Campus, 11380 NW 27th Ave., Building 3, Conference Center Room 3249.  RSVP: [www.ypnmiami.com/featured/intro-to-coding-workshop](http://www.ypnmiami.com/featured/intro-to-coding-workshop)

**[![Rasperrypi](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2019b00465ed8970d-320wi.jpg "Rasperrypi")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019b00465ed8970d-pi)Programming for Non-Programmers:** This is a series of workshops on programming for non-programmers by Feynlabs' Ajit Jaokar at The Lab Miami in Wynwood. The sessions are based on pioneering work by Feynlabs in UK and Europe on accelerating the learning of programming and computational thinking.

The details: October 30: Programming for Non Programmers | Adults;

October 31: Programming for Non Programmers | Educators;

November 2: Learn programming rapidly using Raspberry Pi | Young People

Cost: $25 ($20 with discount code: digestpi); Free for Students

More info and to register: <http://labraspberrypiworkshops.eventbrite.com/>
