# Commenting procedure changing on Herald blogs, including #StartingGate

*I don't expect this change to affect Starting Gate commenting as all of you have Facebook accounts and typically don't hide behind aliases! I still love ya and hope the comments & discussion on this blog continue. And as always, I welcome guest posts as well as your feedback! Nancy*

**Change in commenting on Miami Herald blogs**

Starting Tuesday, to comment on this blog you’ll need to log in with a Facebook account.

We encourage lively discussion on the blog, but also believe that respect and civility must be part of good debate.

This change brings commenting on MiamiHerald.com blogs in line with our policy on the rest of our website. [We made that change in February 2013](http://www.miamiherald.com/2013/02/09/3226188/from-the-editor-the-quest-for.html).

Your blog posts will carry the name on your Facebook account; you will have the choice of whether your post also appears on your own Facebook page.

Rick Hirsch

Managing Editor

Miami Herald
