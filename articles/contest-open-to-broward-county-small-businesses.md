# Contest open to Broward County small businesses

Trueba Media is running a contest for small business owners in Broward County. The winner gets a 30-second commercial for his or her business, says Luis Trueba. To enter, businesses should go to its Facebook page, press the 'like' button and comment about their business. The winner will be chosen randomly Jan. 31 at noon.

For more information: <http://www.truebamedia.com/small-business-video-contest/>
