# Deadline approaching for pitch contest for Americas-focused female entrepreneurs

[IDB MIF](http://www.fomin.org/) and [NXTPLabs](http://www.nxtplabs.com/) have teamed up on a [Pitch Competition](http://www.fundacity.com/application?id=180) that will choose the most innovative and dynamic women entrepreneurs from Latin America and the Caribbean. This contest will be held as part of the second edition of [WeXchange](http://www.wexchange.co/), a conference to promote the development of Latin American women entrepreneurs and to help them to take their businesses to the next level.

[WeXchange](http://www.wexchange.co/) will take place in Miami on December 11-12. Any startup focused in the Latin American and/or Caribbean market that has technology and innovation as its key components, that has at least one female co-founder, and that has received less than $US 1 million in funding is eligible to participate in the [Pitch Competition](http://www.fundacity.com/application?id=180).

**This contest is open to South Florida-based startups with female founders as long as your startup’s focus is on Latin America or the Caribbean marketplace.**

Women entrepreneurs interested in participating should fill out the [application form](http://www.fundacity.com/application?id=180) by **August 20**.
