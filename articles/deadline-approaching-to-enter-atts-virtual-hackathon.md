# Deadline approaching to enter AT&T's Virtual Hackathon

A Virtual Hackathon that AT&T is hosting with $35,000 in cash prizes will only be accepting submissions until 11:59PM PT on Monday March 31th, 2014. This contest is open to new app ideas and for current start-ups that are interested in integrating our AT&T U-verse enabled APIs. The top 10 pitches will then be selected and given 1 month to integrate one or more of the APIs into a fully functioning app; with 4 of these 10 winning a cash prize. Top apps will have the chance for marketing support. AT&T U-verse developer kits will be provided to devs that do not have access to a functioning U-verse box for development. Visit [http://goo.gl/QLosOM](http://cl.exct.net/?ju=fe2f15797160067d771777&ls=fdcd1572756c0d7d741d727566&m=fef51678726c05&l=fe99157274670c7f70&s=fe0b1572756c047c77147170&jb=ffcf14&t=) to submit your pitch now. Multiple pitches can be submitted, but no more than one pitch per person may be selected as a finalist.
