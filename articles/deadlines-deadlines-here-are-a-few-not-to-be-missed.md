# Deadlines, deadlines -- here are a few not to be missed

[SIME MIA](http://simemia.co/) on Dec. 3 and 4 promises to be a unique event featuring dozens of big names in tech, including Caterina Fake, CEO of Findery, co-founder of Flickr and Hunch, and founding partner of Founder Collective; Navin Thukkaram, founder and former COO of Qwiki, which was sold to Yahoo! in July; and Francisco Alvarez, founder and partner of Riverwood Capital Partners. If SIME is on your wish list but the price tag is giving you pause, there’s hope: You could win two free tickets, as well as a one-on-one session with an international investor.

To apply as a [Knight Foundation Entrepreneurial Fellow](enter-knight-entrepreneurial-fellows-contest-for-chance-to-win-free-tickets-to-sime-mia.md), make a 60 second (or less) video on why you should win and be creative. Upload it to Instagram, Facebook and/or YouTube by 11:59 p.m. **Nov. 20.** You must mention/tag @simemiami and @knightfdn and share it as much as possible on social media platforms using #SIMEMIA. Ten winners will be announced on Nov. 27. More info: **[Simemia.co](http://www.simemia.co/).**

Here are a few other deadlines entrepreneurs won’t want to miss:

**Nov. 22:** Last day to apply for the first class of Miami Dade College’s 10,000 Small Businesses, the free 12-week program of classes, workshops, mentoring and networking designed to help small businesses grow. Find the application on: [www.mdc.edu/10ksb](http://www.mdc.edu/10ksb).

**Nov. 27:** Deadline to apply to present at the Florida Venture Capital Conference in January in Orlando, in front of hundreds of VCs and angels. More info: [http://www.flventure.org](http://www.flventure.org/).
