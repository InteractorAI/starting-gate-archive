# EDC leaving Research Park at FAU

**By Marcia Heroux Pounds,** **[Sun Sentinel](https://web.archive.org/web/20131118032835/mailto:Sun%20Sentinel)**

As Enterprise Development Corp.(EDC) showcases local emerging businesses and technologies Thursday, its chief executive Rob Strandberg is getting ready to move its offices.

After 13 years, the nonprofit, which assists startup companies, and its current landlord the Research Park at [Florida Atlantic University](http://../topic/education/colleges-universities/florida-atlantic-university-OREDU0000109.topic) in [Boca Raton](http://www.sun-sentinel.com/community/news/bocaraton?track=tax-bocaraton), are parting ways. EDC soon plans to relocate potentially to [Broward County](http://www.sun-sentinel.com/news/local/broward/), Strandberg said Monday. Possible locations include TechWalk in [Coral Springs](http://../topic/us/florida/broward-county/coral-springs-PLGEO100100403030000.topic) or a new incubator opening at [Broward College](http://../topic/education/colleges-universities/broward-college-OREDU000088.topic).
