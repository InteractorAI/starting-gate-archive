# Elebev a semi-finalist in national contest with $280K in prizes

[![Elebev_logo_final_Large](https://web.archive.org/web/20131007130813im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019affc519cb970c-250wi "Elebev_logo_final_Large")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019affc519cb970c-pi)

Here’s news to raise a glass to: Miami-based [Elebev](http://www.elebev.com/) was selected as one of 25 semi-finalists for the [Miller Lite Tap the Future Business plan competition](http://www.mltapthefuture.com/), with the chance of winning up to $280,000in cash. Elebev’s first product, Sfiro Premium Chillers, creates transparent ice spheres that are served in a glass without need for physical contact and worries about contamination.

The competition includes a People’s Pick video competition, ongoing now through Nov. 8, with a $10,000 prize at stake. Vote Here: <http://mltapthefuture.com/Home/Vote> (you can vote up to once per day.)

[![Sfiro-11](https://web.archive.org/web/20131007130813im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019affc4fdbb970b-250wi "Sfiro-11")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019affc4fdbb970b-pi)

There will also be live pitching event Oct. 22 to a set of judges including the Shark Tank’s Daymond John, with $20,000 awarded to five winners. The top five companies from that event will compete in December for a $250,000 cash prize.

Elebev, located at MEC261, recently closed its second round of funding for $400,000, bringing its total raised to $700,000. The company has recently hired a marketing director and a brand ambassador and is getting ready to pilot test its first product in November, said Nestor Villalobos, founder and CEO of Elebev. Cheers!
