# eMerge Americas Techweek opens registration for Launch competition

[![Emergelogo](https://web.archive.org/web/20140308162500im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a73d889a57970d-300wi "Emergelogo")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a73d889a57970d-pi)

South Florida entrepreneurs, here's an opportunity to compete in a regional pitch competition and possibly win stage time (and cash and prizes) at eMerge Americas.

[eMerge Americas Techweek](http://www.emergeamericas.org/) opened registration on Thursday for early-stage and later-stage companies in the Americas region to apply to compete at the inaugural eMerge Techweek Launch competition during eMerge Americas Techweek in May, said Xavier Gonzalez, executive director of the Technology Foundation of the Americas, which is putting on the conference.

Semi-finalists from throughout the Americas and representing both early and later stage enterprises will compete and present live at the eMerge Americas Techweek expo May 5-6. Winners in each track, selected by a panel of judges, will be awarded with prizes totaling $150,000 in cash and in-kind services from companies such as Greenberg Traurig and Rackspace.
