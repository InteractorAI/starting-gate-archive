# Entrepreneurs' Organization holding its global conference in Miami

The South Florida chapter of [Entrepreneurs’ Organization](http://www.eonetwork.org/) will host the global association’s 2013 ***University*** conference March 13-17, attracting more than 300 EO entrepreneurs representing about 30 countries to the Fontainebleau Hotel on Miami Beach and at other venues throughout the  
region.

Daymond John of FIBU and ABC’s *Shark Tank* and Manny Medina, of Medina Capital and founder of Terremark, are two of the confirmed speakers, organizers said. A final roster of presenters, events and special guests while be announced in coming weeks. Recent EO ***University*** conferenceswere held in Amsterdam, Netherlands; Cape Town, South Africa; and Istanbul, Turkey.

“It’s a big honor for our chapter and the region to host this event,” said Aaron Lee, president of Miami Lakes-based Illuminati Studios and a chairman on the ***University*** planning committee.  “In addition to the revenue the event will generate for the area, it’s a great opportunity to show business leaders from all over the world the strong entrepreneurial spirit and resources we have throughout South Florida.”

EO-SOFLO is the global group’s fourth-largest chapter, with more than 150 entrepreneur members in Miami-Dade, Broward and Palm Beach.  EO-SOFLO businesses account for more than $1 billion in revenues and 25,000 jobs in this region, according to the chapter. Globally, [Entrepreneurs’ Organization](http://www.eonetwork.org/) has more than 8,000 members – all majority shareholders businesses with at least $1 million in revenue -- in more than 40 countries.
