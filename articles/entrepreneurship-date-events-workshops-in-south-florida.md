# Entrepreneurship Datebook: Events, workshops in South Florida

**[![Tech egg](https://web.archive.org/web/20150204084505im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c7432e53970b-120wi "Tech egg")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c7432e53970b-pi)GIRL DEVELOP IT:** Meet other women in technology, share advice and build a Coral Gables Girl Develop It community, 7 p.m. Monday, Feb. 2, Sergio’s 3252 Coral Way. Free. More info: <http://www.meetup.com/Girl-Develop-It-Miami/>

**CODE FOR MIAMI:** Participate in civic software projects for the social good, 7 p.m. Mondays, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St., Wynwood. More info: [thelabmiami.com](http://www.thelabmiami.com/) (click on events)

**ALL ABOUT LEASING**: SCORE Miami-Dade presents a workshop on “Leasing: What You Don't Know Could Hurt Your Business,” 6 p.m.-8 p.m. Tuesday, Feb. 3, Miami Innovation Center, 1951 NW Seventh Ave., third floor. More info: [miamidade.score.org](http://www.miamidade.score.org/) or [here.](http://events.r20.constantcontact.com/register/event?llr=ry4vprn6&oeidk=a07eabatxwq63ecc8ba)

**GROWTH CONFERENCE:** Barbara Corcoran of TV’s “Shark Tank” is the keynote speaker at Entrepreneur Magazine’s free Growth Conference, with workshops on growth strategy, branding and raising capital, 7:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. Wednesday, Feb. 4, Miami Beach Convention Center, Palm Ballroom. Register: [www.entrepreneur.com/growthcon](http://www.entrepreneur.com/growthcon)

**SOUTH FLORIDA CODE CAMP 2015:** Code camp is a free developer community conference, 7:30 a.m. to 5:45 p.m. Saturday, Feb. 7, Nova Southeastern University, Davie. More info: <http://www.fladotnet.com/codecamp/>

**COMING UP - BUSINESS PLAN BOOTCAMP:** Hear from the experts what should be in a great short business plan and pitch to investors, 6:30 to 8:30 p.m., Feb. 18, Miami Dade College Chapman Conference Center (building 3, 2nd floor). Free. Sign up here: <http://hrld.us/bootcamp>.
