# Entrepreneurship Datebook: A sampling of events, workshops in South Florida

**[![Tech egg](https://web.archive.org/web/20150219002543im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c74d7ce8970b-120wi "Tech egg")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c74d7ce8970b-pi)CODE FOR FORT LAUDERDALE:** Civic hackers welcome at OpenTechNight at General Provision, 6:30 p.m. Wednesday, 525 NW First Ave., Fort Lauderdale. codeforftl.org

**MIT@MDC:** A talk about “Innovation Happens Everywhere,” a talk by Jose Estabil, director of Entrepreneurship & Innovation at the MIT Skoltech Initiative, noon to 2 p.m. Wednesday, Feb. 18, at the Idea Center at Miami Dade College. More info: <https://www.eventbrite.com/e/mitmdc-innovation-happens-everywhere-jose-estabil-mit-tickets-15451660332>

**BUSINESS PLAN BOOTCAMP:** Hear from the experts on what should be in a great short business plan and pitch to investors, 6:30 to 8:30 p.m. Wednesday, Feb. 18, at Miami Dade College Chapman Conference Center (building 3, 2nd floor). Free but sign up at <http://hrld.us/bootcamp>.

**INNOVATION & ENGINEERING WEEKEND:** A series of events for all ages at the Patricia and Phillip Frost Museum of Science with the theme “Building the City of the Future,” Thursday through Sunday, Friday, Feb. 19-22. The events culminate with a weekend festival of technology, green solutions, and interactive activities. [www.miamisci.org/iefrost](http://www.miamisci.org/iefrost) or call 305-646-4200.

**I HACKED MIAMI HACKATHON:** A hackathon for the civic good, I <Hacked> Miami CodeAcross 2015, will take place from 9 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Saturday, Feb. 21, at [Miami Ad School](http://www.miamiadschool.com/advertising-school/miami), 571 NW 28th St., Miami. To sign up: [meetup.com/Code-for-Miami/events/219794137](http://www.meetup.com/Code-for-Miami/events/219794137/)

**UHACK HACKATHON:** College and high school students across South Florida are invited to compete in UHack Saturday and Sunday, Feb. 21-22 at the University of Miami Student Activities Center. Theme: Making life better. To register: <http://coe.miami.edu/uhack2015/>

**TWO SCORE WORKSHOPS: SCORE** Miami-Dade presents **“**Building Your Brand,” 9:30 to noon, and “Protecting Your Tradmarks,” 12:30 to 2:30, both on Saturday, Feb. 21, The Innovation Center at UMLSTP, 1951 NW 7th Ave., 1st floor, Miami. More info: [miamidade.score.org](http://miamidade.score.org/)

**BLACK TECH WEEK:** A weeklong series of events celebrating innovators of color and tech innovation with speakers from around the globe, Feb. 23-28, Miami Dade College North Campus. More info and tickets: [blacktechweek.com](http://www.blacktechweek.com/)
