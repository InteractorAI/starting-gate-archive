# Entrepreneurship Datebook: Business Plan Bootcamp, other events this week in South Florida

TextEditor

FREE BOOTCAMP MONDAY

**Miami Herald Business Plan Bootcamp:** Hear from the experts about best practices for starting and growing a business, writing a short business plan and raising capital, 6-8 p.m. Monday, March 7, at Miami Dade College’s Wolfson campus, 300 NE Second Ave., Building 1, Room 1261 (auditorium on second floor). Bring plenty of business cards for networking; there will also be a raffle for co-working space at WeWork. Check-in begins at 5:30 p.m. Free parking in the Miami Dade College garage at 500 NE Second Ave. (entrances are on Northeast Fifth and Sixth streets).  Registration is closed, but **walk-ins are welcome**. [More info here.](http://www.miamiherald.com/news/business/biz-monday/article63024512.html)
