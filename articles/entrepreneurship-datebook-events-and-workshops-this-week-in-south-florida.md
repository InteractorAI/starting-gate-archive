# Entrepreneurship datebook: Events and workshops this week in South Florida

**

[![Tech egg](https://web.archive.org/web/20141224120417im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c71240c6970b-120wi "Tech egg")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c71240c6970b-pi)

SIME MIA:** A gathering of global thought leaders from technology, media and the arts, including Jimmy Maymann, CEO of Huffington Post, Bobby Moresco, Oscar-winning screenwriter for Crash and Million Dollar Baby, Matt Mahan of Brigade and Mike Butcher of TechCrunch, Monday-Wednesday, Dec. 1-3, New World Symphony Center and The LAB Miami. Tickets: [Simemia.co](http://www.simemia.co/). Read more about [Sime MIA here.](sime-mia-promises-thought-provoking-talks-entertainment-surprises.md)

**CITI FINTECH MEETUP:** New meetup group with panel on “Startup Opportunities: Where Miami Can Win?” at 6:30 p.m. Wednesday, Dec. 3, at The LAB Miami. Free but must register at [Simemia.co](http://www.simemia.co/).

**ITPALOOZA:** With more than 2,000 registrants, 60 speakers, 65 sponsors and 50 user groups from across South Florida, this third annual all-day event for tech professionals will feature a hiring fair, hackathon, workshops, exhibits and holiday party, Thursday, Dec. 4, Nova Southeastern, 3301 College Ave, Davie. ITPalooza also raises money for various South Florida nonprofits including Miami-Dade County Public Schools’ STEM Advisory Board, The Stockdale Foundation and Marine’s Toy for Tots. Tickets are free with toy donations: [itpalooza.com](http://www.itpalooza.com/). Read [more about ITPalooza here.](with-more-than-2000-registrants-60-speakers-65-sponsors-and-50-user-groups-from-across-south-florida-the-3rd-annual-itpal.md)

**JURNID SESSIONS:** Art Basel Edition: Media Arts & Verticals, 6:30 to 8 p.m., Thursday at The LAB Miami. More info: [thelabmiami.com](https://www.thelabmiami.com/) (click on Events)

**CODE FOR FORT LAUDERDALE:** Civic hacking group meets at OpenTechNight @ NSU, 6:30 to 8:30 p.m. Thursday, Dec. 4, Nova Southeastern, Davie. More info: [www.meetup.com/Code-for-FTL](http://www.meetup.com/Code-for-FTL)

Biscayne Blvd Seventh Floor, (Ross Dept. Store Building), Miami.
