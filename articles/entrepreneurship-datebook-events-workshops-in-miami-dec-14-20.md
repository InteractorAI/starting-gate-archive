# Entrepreneurship Datebook: Events, workshops in Miami Dec. 14-20

**![Tech egg](https://web.archive.org/web/20151218065012im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d183a94b970c-120wi "Tech egg")CODE FOR MIAMI:** Join civic hackers, writers, government staff, developers, designers and and others interested in civic hacking, 7 p.m. Monday, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St. More info: thelabmiami.com

**#WAFFLEWEDNESDAY:** Join the Miami tech community and meet startups for  waffles and networking, 9 a.m. Wednesday, LiveNinja HQ, 120 NW 25th St, Miami. [More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/waffle-wednesday-tickets-15293102080)

**CS50X OPEN HOUSE:** With the next CS40x coding course coming up, Miami Dade College’s Idea Center presents a “Land Your Dream Job in Technology” open house to offer information about the course experience, provide guidance for the assessment portion of the application, and answer any questions, 6 p.m. Wednesday, 315 NE 2nd Avenue, Building 8 5th Floor[. More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/land-your-dream-job-in-technology-cs50x-miami-open-house-tickets-19823888785?mc_cid=1147ead4ca&mc_eid=5be1f35b59)

**OFFICE HOURS BLACK TECH MEETUP:** Hear a discussion by creative leaders in tech and entrepreneurship, 7 p.m. Thursday, CIC Miami at UM Life Science and Technology Park, 1951 NW 7th Ave., Miami. [More info here.](http://blacktechweek.com/event/office-hours-black-tech-week-meetup/)

**IRONHACK HACKSHOW:** IRONHACK Miami's 4th Cohort shares final projects and the coding school celebrates its first anniversary in Miami, 6:30 p.m. Thursday, BUILDING.Co, 120 SW 8th Street, Miami. [More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/hackshow-by-ironhack-tickets-19521918584)

**CREATIVE MORNINGS MIAMI:** Natalia Martinez-Kalinina, general manager of Cambridge Innovation Center Miami and founder of Awesome Foundation’s chapter in Miami, talks about “Time” at the monthly event, 8:30 a.m. Friday, Vizcaya Museum and Gardens. [More info here.](https://creativemornings.com/talks/natalia-martinez-kalinina?mc_cid=81f746b5ff&mc_eid=c1405bd67b)
