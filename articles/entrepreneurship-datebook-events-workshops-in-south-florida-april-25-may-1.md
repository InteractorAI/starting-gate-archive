MarketHack:** The digital marketing training program presents Tricks of the Twitter Trade, 5:30 p.m. to 7:30 p.m., Monday, Idea Center at Miami Dade College, Wolfson campus, fifth floor. [More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/making-your-idea-into-a-prototype-tickets-24406691070)

**Brainfood with Zumba:** The Brainfood speaker series presents “The Zumba Case” with CEO Alberto Perlman, 6:30 p.m. to 9 p.m. Thursday, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St., Wynwood. [More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/brainfood-with-endeavor-lab-miami-featuring-alberto-perlman-founder-of-zumba-fitness-tickets-24442651629)

[More info and ticket information here.](http://events.r20.constantcontact.com/register/event?oeidk=a07ecf1gsxt5d2c2e0c&llr=npqv7aqab)
