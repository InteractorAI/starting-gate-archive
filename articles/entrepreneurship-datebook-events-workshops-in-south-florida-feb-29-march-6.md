# Entrepreneurship Datebook: Events, workshops in South Florida Feb. 29-March 6

**![Tech egg](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d1a59d8f970c-120wi.jpg "Tech egg")Brainfood:** Speaker series features Seth Schachner, managing director of Strat Americas, discussing the intersection of tech and music, 6:30to 8:30 p.m. Tuesday, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St., Wynwood. [More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/brainfood-with-endeavor-the-lab-miami-featuring-seth-schachner-tickets-21684371539)

**Tomorrow Tour:** Join Technical.ly and Comcast NBCUniversal for a multi-city event and reporting series to inspire discussions about what’s possible when technology and innovation converge to create stronger cities, 5 to 7:30 p.m. Wednesday, Newman Alumni Center, University of Miami, 6200 San Amaro Dr., Coral Gables. More info: [http://tomorrow-Tour.ticketleap.com/miami](http://tomorrow-tour.ticketleap.com/miami).

**The Startup Dating Game:** Northside Innovation Meetup will feature a pitch contest of South Florida startups, 6:30 to 9:30 p.m. Wednesday, WeWork, 350 Lincoln Rd., Miami Beach. [Register on Eventbrite here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/northside-innovation-meetup-miami-powered-by-dell-microsoft-tickets-21676645430)

**Small Business Expo:** Trade show and conference, with networking, workshops and exhibits. Shark Tank’s casting team will be there too, 9:30 a.m. to 4 p.m. Thursday, Miami Beach Convention Center. Free. More info: [www.thesmallbusinessexpo.com](http://www.thesmallbusinessexpo.com/miami).

**Hack-for-Good:** Refresh Miami, Wyncode and Code for Miami host Hack-For-Good, a hackathon that pairs local nonprofits with top tech talent to come up with tech solutions to their biggest challenges, Friday through Sunday, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St., Wynwood. More info and to sign up: [refreshmiami.com](http://www.refreshmiami.com/).
