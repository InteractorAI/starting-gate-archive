# Entrepreneurship Datebook, Events, workshops in South Florida Oct. 26-Nov. 1

**[![Tech egg](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201bb08868b35970d-120wi.jpg "Tech egg")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201bb08868b35970d-pi)**

**UX ESSENTIALS FOR STARTUPS:** Kerry Thompson and Dan Zola from SwayUX explain principles of designing meaningful mobile app and web experiences, 5-7 p.m. Monday, Venture Hive, 1010 NE 2nd Ave., Miami. More info: <https://miami.venturehive.com/dashboard/home>

**IDEA CENTER SPEAKER SERIES:** Local tech entrepreneur Eliam Medina will share his experience at the prestigious Y Combinator, 7-8:30 p.m. Monday, Idea Center at Miami Dade College, Wolfson Campus, Building 8/fifth floor. [More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/notes-from-silicon-valley-pioneersmdc-eliam-medina-tickets-17765950436?ref=enivte001&invite=ODYzNjYzMS9uZGFobGJlcmdAbWlhbWloZXJhbGQuY29tLzA%3D&utm_source=eb_email&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=invitemodernv2&ref=enivte001&utm_term=attend)

**NORTHSIDE INNOVATION MEETUP:** Hear a discussion by leaders and innovators in Miami’s growing music scene at this meetup by Dell and Intel, 6:30 to 9:30 p.m. Tuesday, 360Spaces, 220 NW 27th St., Miami. More info on Eventbrite. [More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/northside-innovation-meetup-miami-powered-by-dell-intel-tickets-19062225630)

**#W3RTECH LAUNCH:** Join Digital Grass Innovation & Technology for the launch of #W3RTech, a photo display of community leaders and activists, entrepreneurs and employees of South Florida’s tech and innovation community that illustrates the diversity and inclusion of the ecosystem, 6-9 p.m. Wednesday, WeWork, 350 Lincoln Road, Miami Beach. Free. More info: <http://w3rtech.com/w3rtech-events/>
