# Entrepreneurship Datebook: Events, workshops this week in South Florida

**[![Tech egg](https://web.archive.org/web/20150424123559im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d0a3994d970c-120wi "Tech egg")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d0a3994d970c-pi)STARTUP GRIND:** The monthly meetup features an interview with Will Weinraub, founder of LiveNinja, 6-9 p.m. Tuesday, Venture Hive, 1010 NE Second Ave, Miami. Fee applies. More info: [www.startupgrind.com/miami](http://www.startupgrind.com/miami)

**GRANT FUNDING:** SCORE Miami-Dade presents “Acquiring Grant Funds for Your Business,” 6-8 p.m. Wednesday, Innovation Center at UM Life Science and Technology Park, 1951 NW Seventh Ave., third floor, Miami. Fee applies. More info: [miamidade.score.org](http://miamidade.score.org/) (click on local workshops)

**BOOK SIGNING:** Linda Rottenberg, co-founder of Endeavor, will be discuss ng and sign ng her new book on entrepreneurship, “Crazy is a Compliment,” 6-7:30 p.m., Tuesday, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St., Miami. [More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/crazy-is-a-compliment-book-signing-tickets-14383488401)

**WOMEN’S SUCCESS SUMMIT:** Miami’s largest business conference for entrepreneurial women will be Wednesday and Thursday at The Event Center, 137 NE 19th St., Miami. Fee applies. More info: [womenssuccesssummit.com](http://www.womenssuccesssummit.com/)

**BRAINFOOD @ THE LAB:** Speaker series features Mark Kingdon, an early stage investor in Twitter and former head of Linden Labs (Second Life), 6:30-8:30 p.m., Thursday, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St., Wynwood. Fee applies. More info: [thelabmiami.com](http://www.thelabmiami.com/) (click on events)

**WEXCHANGE 2014:** This two-day conference to promote Latin American women entrepreneurs and offer networking, education, a pitch competition and opportunities to meet with investors, will be Thursday and Friday at Miami Dade College and The LightBox. Fees apply. Info: [wexchange.co.](http://www.wexchange.co/)
