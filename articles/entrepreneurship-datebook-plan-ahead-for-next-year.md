# Entrepreneurship Datebook: Plan ahead for next year

![Techegg](https://web.archive.org/web/20151226040610im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201bb08a18a8b970d-120wi "Techegg")Entrepreneurship Datebook highlights educational workshops and networking events each week. With the holiday week quiet on the event and networking front, here are a few entrepreneurship events to put on your calendars for next year:

**Black Tech Week:** Back for a second year, a week of events with speakers, panels, workshops and networkers, Feb. 14-20. More info: [www.blacktechweek.com](http://www.blacktechweek.com/)

**SUP-X:** A startup expo for entrepreneurs, investors and service providers, Feb. 16-17, Broward County Convention Center. More info: [www.sup-x.org](http://www.sup-x.org/).

**Miami Mini Maker Faire:** The popular event returns in an expanded format Feb. 20-21 at YoungArts in Miami. MIAMade is now accepting applications for exhibitors on its website. More info: [makerfairemiami.com](http://makerfairemiami.com/)

**eMerge Americas**: The third annual eMerge Americas conference, with two days of speakers, exhibits, contests and celebrity fun, returns April 18-19. eMerge is accepting applications for its Startup Showcase now on its website. More info: [emergeamericas.org](http://emergeamericas.org/).

**Startup Acceleration**: Powered by Consero, this calls itself a invitation-only peer-to-peer event for startup founders and it’s June 8-10. More info and to ask for an invite: [startup.consero.com](http://startup.consero.com/)
