STARTING GATE

Startups and early-stage tech companies have been raising funds: MDLIVE, a telehealth provider in Sunrise, [closed on a $15 million private equity round](mdlive-closes-on-50-million-of-private-equity-funding.md) of funding, following news of Miami-based TissueTech, a biotech focusing on regenerative medicine, [raising $15 million](funding-news-tissuetech-raises-15-million-.md). Also in the news: [Instacart doubled its delivery area](instacart-marches-north-doubles-delivery-area-in-miami-dade.md) in South Florida, and [Waleteros launched](waleteros-launches-app-for-underbanked-on-google-play.md) its app for the underbanked in the U.S.
