# Entrepreneurship Datebook: Workshops, events for week of Dec. 7-13

**

[![Tech egg](https://web.archive.org/web/20211002045643im_/https://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c7f5f54f970b-120wi "Tech egg")](https://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c7f5f54f970b-pi)

TECHWEEK MIAMI:** Activities through Sunday will include the national finals of the Launch startup competition on Thursday with startup champions from Miami and five other cities. Another featured event is [Hatch Match Miami](http://thelabmiami.com/event/hatch-match-miami-2015/) on Tuesday. Tickets and info: [techweek.com/miami](http://www.techweek.com/miami).

**SPEAKER SERIES:** The Jim Moran Institute for Global Entrepreneurship’s Advice Straight Up speaker series features Phyllis Newhouse, CEO and co-founder of Xtreme Solutions, 8 to 10 a.m. Tuesday at the Urban League of Broward County in Fort Lauderdale. More info: [www.advicestraightup.com](http://www.advicestraightup.com/).

**INCUBATOR SHOWCASE:** Meet the startups in Venture Hive’s Fall incubator SWARM! Showcase, sponsored by DLA Piper, 5 to 8 p.m. Tuesday, Venture Hive, 1010 NE Second Ave., Miami. [RSVP here](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/venture-hive-and-dla-piper-present-fall-incubator-swarm-showcase-tickets-19661831066) directly or <https://miami.venturehive.com/dashboard/home>.

**STARTUP GRIND:** The monthly interview features tech entrepreneur/angel investor Mark Kingdon, 6 to 9 p.m. Tuesday at WeWork Miami Beach, 350 Lincoln Road. Tickets: <https://www.startupgrind.com/miami/>.

**WIT RENDEZVOUS:** eMerge Americas WIT features panel discussion on “Thoughts to Actions: Locals Making a Change,” 6:30 to 8:30 p.m. Tuesday, The Standard hotel. [RSVP here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/women-innovation-technology-wit-rendez-vous-10-thoughts-to-actions-locals-making-a-change-tickets-19638208410?mc_eid=d537e17ef3&mc_cid=39dc023256)

**WHAT’S NEXT SPEAKER SERIES:** Jim McKelvey, co-founder of Square and LaunchCode, will discuss his experiences in computer programming, creative problem solving, and entrepreneurship, 6 to 8 p.m. Thursday, The Idea Center, Wolfson Campus, Building 8, 5th Floor. [RSVP here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/whats-next-speaker-series-with-jim-mckelvey-tickets-19823859698?mc_eid=5be1f35b59&mc_cid=044e38afcc)

**THE NEW NEWS ON CROWDFUNDING:** Krillion Ventures’ Melissa Krinzman interviews Ryan Feit, CEO of SeedInvest, a leading equity crowdfunding platform with 13,500 investors, 7:30 p.m. to 9 p.m. Thursday, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St. [RSVP required here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/the-new-news-on-equity-crowdfunding-tickets-19822023205)
