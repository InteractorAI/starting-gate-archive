# Entrepreneurship Datebook: Workshops, events in South Florida

Practice Your Pitch:** WeWork Lincoln Road and itabo host a practice five-minute demo day pitch competition, and the reward will be feedback, 1 p.m. Tuesday, WeWork, 350 Lincoln Road. [More info here.](https://www.refreshmiami.com/event/practice-pitch-hosted-itabo/)

**Pioneers@MDC:** Join Daniel Rodriguez, founder, data scientist and roboticist behind Miami-based Animusoft, as he explores where these technologies are today and where we will be in the future, 6 to 8 p.m. Tuesday, Idea Center at Miami Dade College, Wolfson Campus, Building 8, fifth floor. [More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/pioneersmdc-with-daniel-rodriguez-founder-of-aliveai-tickets-19917282127?aff=es2)

**Start-Up City with author Gabe Klein:** The author and advisor to tech and transportation startups will discuss ways cities can better address challenges such as urban mobility, 6 p.m. to 8 p.m. Wednesday, Idea Center at Miami Dade College, Wolfson Campus, Building 8, fifth floor. [More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/talk-by-gabe-klein-transit-innovator-and-author-of-start-up-city-tickets-20036249963?aff=efbnreg)

**Coding Class Info Session:** Meet CodePRO instructors and alumni, who will share success stories and answer your questions about the upcoming class offerings based on Harvard’s CS50x course, 6 p.m. to 8 p.m. Thursday, Idea Center at Miami Dade College, Wolfson Campus, Building 8, fifth floor. [More info here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/land-your-dream-job-in-technology-cs50x-miami-orientation-tickets-20588004275?aff=es2)

**Startup Grind:** Brandon Timinsky, founder and CEO of GasNinjas, an on-demand fuel service, is the featured interview, 6 p.m. to 9 p.m. Jan. 19 (date was changed) at WeWork Miami Beach, 350 Lincoln Road. More info: [Startupgrind.com/Miami](http://goo.gl/4vgOEU)

**Women in Tech:** Refresh Miami and Citi present an event about women in tech featuring Mary Biggins of ClassPass and Brodi Jackson of Caerus Ventures, 6:30 to 9 p.m. Jan. 20, WeWork, 350 Lincoln Road. More info: [Refreshmiami.com](http://www.refreshmiami.com/)
