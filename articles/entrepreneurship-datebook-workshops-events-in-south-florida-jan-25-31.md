# Entrepreneurship Datebook: Workshops, events in South Florida Jan. 25-31

**

[![Tech egg](https://web.archive.org/web/20250911040700im_/https://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c80bb2c7970b-120wi "Tech egg")](https://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c80bb2c7970b-pi)

Code for Miami:** Join civic hackers coding solutions to make government and the city work better, 7 p.m. to 9 p.m. Monday, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St., Wynwood. More info: [TheLabMiami.com](http://www.thelabmiami.com/)

**LaunchCode Presents South Florida Speaks .NET:** In the first in a series of South Florida Speaks events by LaunchCode, a panel of experienced .NET developers will discuss the many ways Microsoft’s C#-based development framework is used in web development, mobile development and cloud computing, 6:30 to 8:30 p.m. Tuesday, The Idea Center at Miami Dade College, Wolfson Campus, Building 8, Fifth Floor. [More info here](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/launchcode-presents-south-florida-speaks-net-tickets-20634836351).

**Brainfood:** The speaker series sponsored by Endeavor Miami features Alexandre Hohagen, an investor and advisor and former VP of Facebook Latin America and U.S. Hispanics, 6:30 to 9 p.m. Tuesday, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St., Wynwood. [Tickets on Eventbrite.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/brainfood-with-endeavor-the-lab-miami-featuring-alexandre-hohagen-tickets-20893176052?aff=es2)

**Latin America Tech Meetup:** Learn about how to get into startup accelerators, in a discussion with speakers from YCombinator, StartupMexico and Win Lab Accelerator, 6:30 to 9 p.m., Tuesday, Building Co, 120 SW Eighth St. [More info on Eventbrite.](http://www.eventbrite.com/e/getting-into-startup-accelerators-with-ycombinator-startupmexico-and-win-lab-accelerator-tickets-20880737849?aff=es2)

**#WaffleWednesday:** Members of Miami’s creative and tech communities are invited to the LiveNinja office in Wynwood to hear pitches and announcements from local startups over coffee and waffles, 9 to 10:30 a.m. Wednesday, LiveNinja, 120 NW 25th Street, Loft #301, Miami. [More info here.](http://www.eventbrite.com/e/waffle-wednesday-tickets-19608336061?aff=eac2)

**Digital Breakfast:** Cybertising and Google Partners are co-presenting their second event to discuss Google advertising, webmaster and analytics solutions and opportunities to ramp up your online presence and ROI, 8:30 to 10:30 a.m. Thursday, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St. [More info on Eventbrite](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/cybertisingtm-google-partners-digital-breakfast-2-the-lab-miami-tickets-20764235387).

**Global Game Jam:** GameTrep hosts a 48-hour [Global Game Jam](http://knightfoundation.us5.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=44a06e103b99cf80e07a2eaad&id=2c3b4dc08e&e=bb27c06725) in Miami, bringing together gaming developers, artists, writers, students and industry leaders, Friday through Sunday, Cidy Desk co-working center, 350 S. Miami Ave., Miami. [More info here.](http://globalgamejam.org/2016/jam-sites/make-games-miami-city-desk)

**Daymond John:** The ABC *Shark Tank* star and founder and CEO of FUBU will be discussing entrepreneurship and his new book, *The Power of Broke*, at 6 p.m. Sunday, Books & Books, 265 Aragon Ave., Coral Gables. [More info here.](http://www.booksandbooks.com/event/daymond-john-power-broke-gables)
