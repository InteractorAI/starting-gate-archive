FUNDINGPOST:** Hear a panel of investors at a FundingPost event, 5:30 to 8:30 p.m., Wednesday, Venture Hive, 1010 NE Second Ave., Miami. Tickets: [fundingpost.com](http://www.fundingpost.com/) (click on events).

**STARTUP GRIND:** The monthly interview series features Adam Smith, partner of Medina Capital, 6 to 9 p.m., Wednesday, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St., Miami. Tickets: [startupgrind.com/miami](http://www.startupgrind.com/miami).

**GOLD COAST VENTURE CAPITAL ASSOCIATION EVENT:** Panel discussion on “Should I Incubate, Accelerate or Co-Locate?” 5:30 to 8 p.m., June 24, Embassy Suites, Boca Raton. Tickets: [gcvca.org](http://www.gcvca.org/)

**DESIGN THINKING:** Learn about design thinking, a process for solving problems and developing business strategy that puts an increased emphasis on understanding your customers, and hear keynote speech by Andy Hagerman of The Design Gym in this Refresh Miami event, 6 to 9 p.m., June 25, Patricia and Phillip Frost Miami Museum of Science, 3280 S. Miami Ave., Miami. Tickets: [refreshmiami.com](http://www.refreshmiami.com/) (click on events).
