# Entrepreneurship Datebook: Workshops, events this week in South Florida

**[![Tech egg](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c7a9bc03970b-120wi.jpg "Tech egg")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c7a9bc03970b-pi)**

**DIGITAL MARKETING:** Venture Hive offers a weekly series of free digital marketing workshops, in partnership with W Academy,  5 p.m. Tuesdays, 1010 NE 2nd Ave., Miami. More info: [welcu.com/wacademy/free-summer-class-by-webcongres](http://welcu.com/wacademy/free-summer-class-by-webcongres)

**MILLER LITE TAP THE FUTURE:** Miller Lite Tap the Future’s Live Pitch Event tour includes networking and startup pitches in front of Daymond John from ABC’s “Shark Tank” and others, 7 p.m. Tuesday, July 7, The Fillmore Miami Beach, 1700 Washington Ave. RSVP: [www.MLTapTheFuture.com](http://www.mltapthefuture.com/)/events.

**CIVIC INNOVATION:** Free networking event and panel discussion on “The Big Business of Civic Innovation” hosted by Digital Grass, with participatory ideation led by Silicon Valley's Carla Mays, 6 to 9 p.m. Wednesday, July 8, The LAB Miami, 400 NW 26th St. More info and RSVP: [digigrass.com](http://www.digigrass.com/).

**CREATE ACCELERATOR SHOWCASE:** See presentations from student-run ventures at the Idea Center’s CREATE Accelerator at Miami Dade College, 5:30 to 8 p.m. Thursday, July 9, The Idea Center, Building 8, fifth floor, MDC Wolfson campus. [Register here.](http://www.eventbrite.com/e/create-accelerator-showcase-tickets-17577838789?mc_eid=5be1f35b59&mc_cid=bd37d09e53)

**BIG DATA:** MDC’s The Idea Center and Instituto de Empresa Business School host a workshop, “Understanding and Using Big Data and Business Analytics,” 6 to 8 p.m. July 16, The Idea Center, Building 8, fifth floor, MDC Wolfson campus. [Register here.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/understanding-and-using-big-data-and-business-analytics-tickets-17326742754)
