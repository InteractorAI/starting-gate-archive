**SOCIAL ENTREPRENEURSHIP:** Momentum International presents Social Entrepreneurship on High Heals with a panel of local leaders, 7 p.m. to 10 p.m. Wednesday, Nov. 5, The LAB Miami. Register here: [socentonhighheels.eventbrite.com](http://socentonhighheels.eventbrite.com/).

**REFRESH MIAMI:** Monthly meetup presents “The Maker Movement” with a discussion on robotics, drones, 3D printing and more, 6:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m. Thursday, Nov. 6, Building 8, Miami Dade College Wolfson campus. Info: [refreshmiami.com](http://www.refreshmiami.com/)

**TIGERDIRECT’S TECHBASH:** TechBash will welcome about 20,000 to Marlins Park from 7 p.m. to 11 p.m. Friday, Nov. 7, for an event that will feature more than 150 vendors, entertainment, food, exhibits, opportunities to purchase the latest technologies at Black Friday-like prices and “many surprises,” say the organizers. Free with advance registration. Info: [tigertechbash.com](http://www.tigertechbash.com/)

**MIAMI MINI MAKER FAIRE:** This all-day, family-friendly celebration of the maker movement is back for a second year in Wynwood, 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. Saturday, Nov. 8, at The LAB Miami, and will include a street festival with about 100 makers, including artists, engineers, entrepreneurs and educators. Tickets: [makerfairemiami.com](http://makerfairemiami.com/)
