# Event: Free small business event Wednesday

To help business owners get on the path to winning government contracts, American Express OPEN is hosting a free event in Miami on Wednesday, May 9. The event, VIP Contract Connections & Teaming, will be held at the InterContinental Hotel-Doral from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Attendees will have access to one-on-one meeting with federal, state and local government buyers and large prime contractors. workshops, keynote speeches and networking. More information: [www.vipgovtcontracting.com](http://www.vipgovtcontracting.com/)
