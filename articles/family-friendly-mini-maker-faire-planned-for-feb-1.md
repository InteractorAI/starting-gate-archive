# Family-friendly Mini-Maker Faire planned for Feb. 1

[![Hamboree_MMF_store - Copy](https://web.archive.org/web/20140122064123im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a5110229a8970c-320wi "Hamboree_MMF_store - Copy")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a5110229a8970c-pi)

Calling all makers and ham radio enthusiasts – the Hamboree Mini-Maker Faire is planned for Saturday, Feb. 1. There is also a Hamfest on Friday, Jan. 31.

A Maker Faire is a family-friendly showcase of invention, creativity and innovation, an event where people show what they are making and share what they are learning. “Makers are crafters, tech enthusiasts, garage tinkerers, scientists and homesteaders. They are of all ages and backgrounds. The goal of this event is to entertain, inform, connect and grow our local Makers community”, said Roberto Cruz, the 2014 Hamboree Mini-Maker Faire chairman.

The Saturday Mini-Maker Faire, which will be combined with a Hamfest for ham radio enthusiasts,  features speakers, workshops and exhibits on topics such as: robotics, 3D printing, home fabrication, green technologies, textile arts, electronics, do-it yourself projects, Amateur Radio communications and related activities as well as a chance buy and sell gadgets, radio gear, computer parts and software. There is also a Hamfest only on Friday.

There will be amateur radio and emergency communications stations setup on the grounds. Come and share your projects or just to look, learn, ask questions, and even get on the microphone with the help of one of our licensed operators. For individuals wishing to receive their FCC Amateur Radio license, exams will be offered onsite Saturday at 8 am.

                  WHAT:     Hamboree Mini-Maker Faire and Hamfest

                  WHEN:     Friday, Jan. 31, noon to 8PM (Hamfest)

                                    Saturday, Feb. 1, 9AM to 6PM (Mini Maker Faire and Hamfest)

                  WHERE: Miami-Dade Fair Expo Center

                                    10901 SW 24 ST (Coral Way)

Attendance is free for students 18 and under; $12 for adults.  If you are a Maker and wish to show off what you have built, inquire with us for a free display table.

For more information about these events, how to participate or how to obtain tickets, please contact the Dade Radio Club at 305-590-8523 or visit [http://hamboreemakerfaire.org](http://hamboreemakerfaire.org/)

The event is sponsored by the Dade Radio Club, which provides logistics support to many local events such as the American Diabetes Tour de Cure, Miami Marathon, MS-150 Bike ride and others.
