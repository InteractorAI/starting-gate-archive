# Final deadline Dec. 6 to apply to present at Florida Venture Capital Conference

Fast growing private Florida companies in all industries are invited to apply to present at the 24th Annual [Florida Venture Capital Conference](http://www.flventure.org/events/2014-florida-venture-capital-conference/presenters-application.aspx?utm_source=Last+Call+for+the+2014+Conference+Presenter+Applications+-+Apply+Today&utm_campaign=FVF), **January 28 - 29, 2014** at the Hyatt Regency (formerly known as The Peabody Orlando).

[CLICK HERE](http://www.flventure.org/events/2014-florida-venture-capital-conference/presenters-application.aspx?utm_source=Last+Call+for+the+2014+Conference+Presenter+Applications+-+Apply+Today&utm_campaign=FVF) to complete the two-page application to apply. But hurry, the deadline is Dec. 6.

The Florida Venture Capital Conference showcases 20-25 later stage, high growth Florida private companies before an international audience of venture capitalists, private equity investors, family offices and other strategic investors. Applicants should have talented management teams, proprietary technology and high growth potential. Although presenters are typically seeking later stage funding, companies not presently seeking funding are welcome to apply.
