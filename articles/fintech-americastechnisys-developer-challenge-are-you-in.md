# Fintech Americas/Technisys Developer Challenge: Are you in?

The IdeaCenter, Fintech Pioneer Technisys and the Fintech Americas Banking Disruption Conference are inviting South Florida Techies with HTML, Javascript + DOM and CSS skills to participate in a Technisys Developer Challenge where you can not only win $3,000, but just might land a job at Technisys, one of the hottest Fintech companies in the Americas.

**[Technisys](http://click.icptrack.com/icp/relay.php?r=16644418&msgid=146246&act=M63R&c=1456605&destination=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.technisys.com%2F)**, is bringing its World Class API’s as well as its top engineers to help South Florida Developers work on a challenge to scout local tech talent while giving the Top 3 Finalist the opportunity to walk away with $3,000 in Prizes!

**WHAT**

Fintech Americas – Technisys Developer Challenge is a collaborative effort betweenFintech Americas and Technisys to help promote the Fintech industry in South Florida.

**WHO**

We are looking for the BEST 100 techies!

If you’re a South Florida techie with HTML, Javascript + DOM and CSS skills and are willing to be part of the banking (r)evolution, sign up for the Technisys – FintechAmericas Hackathon / Challenge this October 16-18 at the Miami Dade College Wolfson Campus’ IdeaCenter!
