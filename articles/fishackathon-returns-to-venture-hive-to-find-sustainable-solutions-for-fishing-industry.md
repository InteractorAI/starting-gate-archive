# Fishackathon returns to Venture Hive to find sustainable solutions for fishing industry

[![Fishackathon Miami](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d11a318c970c-800wi.jpg "Fishackathon Miami")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d11a318c970c-pi)

Venture Hive has partnered once again with the State Department to host the 2nd Annual [Fishackathon](http://fishackathon.co/), the world’s largest hackathon supporting our oceans.

The Fishackathon brings together technologists and coders over a weekend to create tools to address challenges in sustainable fishing around the world. There are 15 cities participating including London, Kuala Lumpur, Vancouver, New York City, Boston and Santiago.Chile.

Miami's [Fishackathon](http://fishackathon.co/) 2015 kicks off on June 5th until June 7th and welcomes hackers, coders and enthusiasts to create solutions that can impact the health of our oceans.

More prizes will be confirmed soon but include a trip to the second annual [Our Ocean's conference](http://www.state.gov/e/oes/ocns/opa/2014conf/index.htm) in Chile this fall and a trip and a chance to present at the [Mobile World Congress](http://www.mobileworldcongress.com/) in Barcelona.
