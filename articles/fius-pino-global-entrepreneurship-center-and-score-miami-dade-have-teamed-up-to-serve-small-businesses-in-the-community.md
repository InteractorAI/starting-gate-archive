# FIU Pino Center, SCORE Miami-Dade team up; intro workshop on Friday

FIU’s [Pino Global
Entrepreneurship Center](http://entrepreneurship.fiu.edu/) and [SCORE Miami-Dade](http://miamidade.score.org/) have teamed up to serve small businesses
in the community, and they are offering a free introductory workshop to be
hosted at FIU's College of Business this Frday, Jan. 25.

The workshop will
include a discussion about how to start all types of businesses and solving
problems in businesses already started and how SCORE can help in both areas. Also
learn about the upcoming events and programs offered by FIU's Pino Global
Entrepreneurship Center including the 2013 Miami Herald Business Plan Challenge
- FIU Track, Entrepreneurship Workshops, Entrepreneur's How To
Webinar Series and its new Start Up Corner.

The seminar will be
Friday, Jan. 26, from 10 a.m. -12:30 p.m. at FIU's College
of Business, Special Events Center, 11200 SW 8th Street, CBC 232 Miami.  [Please register
here](http://r20.rs6.net/tn.jsp?e=001gdtk-5mn9xfbg0SsITb9MtTTl0AKHqN-IhEbOrPcko2uVOaLrxWwMhR2uOm6UyonBo1_nC5kY663f01HAaCU5Z0G0kLSer3MLmTp-oKF1upfSdtKVpVyXvVMKltzxT93yWjWIrmr03behmaWe6WJrFVsn8KkthJskVL8dBmBYw8=).
