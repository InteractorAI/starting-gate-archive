# Florida Venture Forum calls for  early-stage, high-growth companies to  present

On May 15, the [Florida Venture Forum](http;//www.floridaventureforum.org) will host
its Sixth Annual Early Stage Conference at the Reunion Resort in Orlando. The Florida
Venture Forum is now calling for early-stage, high-growth private companies from the state
of Florida to
apply to be presenters at the conference.  To date, presenting companies
from the Forum's Early Stage Conference have raised over $8 millions.

The
principle purpose of the Annual Conference is to showcase about 10-12 companies before a national audience of
venture capitalists, angel investors, private equity investors and investment
bankers. These entrepreneurial companies should have talented management teams,
proprietary technology, high growth potential and should be currently seeking
early stage funding.

[More information is avaiable here](http://www.floridaventureforum.org/subpage.asp?navid=0&id=88), including a link to the
required Executive Summary format ([Presenter Application](http://www.floridaventureforum.org/documents/2013_EarlyStage_Form_distributed.pdf)).
