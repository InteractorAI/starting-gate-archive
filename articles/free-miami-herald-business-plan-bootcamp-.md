# Free Miami Herald Business Plan Bootcamp

Register now for The Miami Herald's free Business Plan Bootcamp on March 6 open to the South Florida community.

We're bringing in expert presenters who have worked with hundreds of entrepreneurs on business plans -- [Melissa Krinzman](http://www.venturearchitects.com/team/melissa-krinzman.shtml) of Venture Architects and Darius Nevin of G3 Capital Partners. Both are also [Business Plan Challenge](http://www.miamiherald.com/challenge) judges this year. By the way, you don't have to enter the Challenge to attend the Bootcamp, but we hope you will.

They will give you the basics on writing a powerful plan that will turn the heads of investors, bankers and judges. There will be a special presentation on presenting financials in your plan -- something I know a lot of entrepreneurs need help with. There will be lots of time in the 90-minute program for Q&A too.

Here are the details:

What: Miami Herald Business Plan Bootcamp

When: 6:30 pm, March 6

Where: Miami Dade College, Wolfson campus, Auditorium (Building 1, second floor, Room 1261)

To Register: Although it's free, space is limited -- register [here](https://extra.herald.com/events/ProductInfo.aspx?EventId=31)

Cost: Did we say it's free? (Parking is also free in the MDC lot at 500 NE 2nd Avenue. It is important to note that the entrances are on NE 5th and 6th Streets.)
