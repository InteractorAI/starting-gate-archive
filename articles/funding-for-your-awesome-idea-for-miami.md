# Apply for a $1,000 grant for your "Awesome" idea for Miami

[![Natalie](https://web.archive.org/web/20121209065634im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017ee5c8b810970d-120wi "Natalie")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017ee5c8b810970d-pi)Natalia Martinez is home again.  She lived in Boston and New York City for the last eight years and returned to South Florida with a refreshed set of eyes to study and peer at Miami.  Martinez didn't want to wait to get connected to the city's civic side.  She brought with her the idea of **[Awesome Foundation](http://www.awesomefoundation.org/)**,  gathered a group of friends and contacts, and developed a **[Miami Chapter](http://www.awesomefoundation.org/en/chapters/miami)** of the organization.  Awesome will be awarding $1,000 funding to ideas that make our local community a stronger, better, more innovative place. Martinez is the "Dean" of the chapter and spoke to **WLRN Miami Herald Business Journalist [Karen Burkett](http://www.wlrn.org/people/karen-burkett)**about the ideas applicants have been submitting.  **[Please listen here.](http://www.miamiherald.com/friday_business_report/) Read an [earlier post here.](ignite-miami-events-always-pack-the-house-there-are-four-4-tedx-groups-in-south-floridaon-monday-ideamensch-httpide.md)**
