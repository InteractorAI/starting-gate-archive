# Global Voice Hall hosting free entrepreneur event at UM on Jan. 21

[![UMping](https://web.archive.org/web/20140122064131im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019b04c42f8f970d-320wi "UMping")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019b04c42f8f970d-pi)[Global Voice Hall](http://www.globalvoicehall.com/) is embarking on a national tour to bring "Rebuilding America: Path of the Entrepreneur" to college campuses, and University of Miami is its first stop. According to the organizers, the goal of the forum is to inspire students with entrepreneurial aspirations and it will include speakers from local Miami businesses and accelerators as well as a pitch competition for students to practice selling their ideas and receive feedback from an experienced panel.

 The local  event will be 5:30 p.m. to 8 p.m. Jan. 21 at the Student Activities Center Ballroom on the UM campus. The programming will begin at 6 and include presentations by [Max Major](http://maxmajor.net/), an entrepreneur and illusionist: CEO and Co-Founder of College Hunks Hauling Junk and University of Miami alum**[Omar Soliman](http://collegehunkshaulingjunk.com/about-us/our-company/bios/omar-soliman);** **and**  **[Marcela Iannini](http://www.startup-miami.com/team-view/marcela/),**board member and executive director of education at Startup Miami; as well as  "*Spotlight on Innovation,*" a [pitch competition presented by Zack Huhn of](http://maxmajor.net/)[Student Intern Network (SIN)](http://www.studentinternnetwork.com/)*,*a startup community of Millennials.

 To find out more or to register for the free event, go here: <http://bit.ly/1cjci9o>. You can also check out the organization at [facebook.com/globalvoicehall](http://facebook.com/globalvoicehall).
