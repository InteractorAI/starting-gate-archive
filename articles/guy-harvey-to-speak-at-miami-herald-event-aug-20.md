# Guy Harvey to speak at Miami Herald event Aug. 20

[![Guyharvey](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201a73e01fd00970d-800wi.jpg "Guyharvey")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a73e01fd00970d-pi)

For fans of Florida fishing and wildlife, artist Guy Harvey is a living legend. He’s also an entrepreneur whose endeavors reach onto dry land in hotels, apparel and books — a story we tell in our Aug. 18 issue of Business Monday.

Find out how he’s expanding his brand at live conversation with Miami Herald reporter Hannah Sampson Aug. 20 at 6 p.m. in the Miami Herald Community Room, 3511 NW 91st Avenue, Doral, 33172. Tickets, $10, at [Http://hrld.us/tickets](http://hrld.us/tickets).
