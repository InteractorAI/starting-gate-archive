# HAUTE DINING launches in South Florida

[![Haute_Dinning_New_LOGO-02](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201a73e0f3f39970d-200wi.jpg "Haute_Dinning_New_LOGO-02")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a73e0f3f39970d-pi)With about 40 eateries already onboard as official partners, including marquee restaurants Lure FIshbar, L’echon Brasserie, Villa Azur, Shikany, and more, the recently launched [HAUTE DINING](http://www.hautedining.com/) culinary program has already been named one of the “must have memberships for foodies” by ZAGAT.

HAUTE DINING members receive a discount of 20% or more at partner restaurants, allowing members to dine out at more restaurants, more frequently, than typical diners. Restaurants are presented via website or iOS or Android smartphone app.

The Miami-based startup is the brainchild of Buzzy Sklar, a veteran culinary entrepreneur, who partnered with Miami Beach marketing expert Christopher Todd and Kevin Frija, previous CEO of Vapor Corporation, to create the concept.

HAUTE DINING memberships are now available for an annual subscription price of $200. Each membership includes designated discounts at partner restaurants, as well as access to events.
