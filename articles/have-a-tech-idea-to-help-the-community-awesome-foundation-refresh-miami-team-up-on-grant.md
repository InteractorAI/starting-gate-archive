# Have a tech idea to help the community? Awesome Foundation, Refresh Miami team up on grant

[![Refreshlogo-11](https://web.archive.org/web/20140808152843im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a73dfc139d970d-120wi "Refreshlogo-11")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a73dfc139d970d-pi)

[Refresh Miami](http://www.refreshmiami.com/) and [Awesome Foundation MIAMI](http://refreshmiami.us5.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=f95e1b250c23dfc0657684983&id=e94a38c919&e=fc8286a6de) have partnered up to award a grant to a startup or entrepreneur who is doing something tech-focused and innovative with an awesome social impact. The only requirement is that the project you are working on leverage technology to positively affect some area of our community.

[![AwesomeFoundation](https://web.archive.org/web/20140808152843im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a511f0e061970c-120wi "AwesomeFoundation")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a511f0e061970c-pi)

Companies and/or individuals selected will have a chance to pitch to the Awesome Foundation board and the winner will be announced during the September Refresh Miami event and will take home a $1,000 grant.

**Deadline is****August 15**! Please include REFRESH on application title. To apply: [http://www.awesomefoundation.org/en/submissions/new?chapter=miami](http://refreshmiami.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=f95e1b250c23dfc0657684983&id=b47e8cd159&e=fc8286a6de)
