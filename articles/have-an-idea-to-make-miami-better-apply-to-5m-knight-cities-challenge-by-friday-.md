# Have an idea to make Miami better? Apply to $5M Knight Cities Challenge by Friday

[![Logo](https://web.archive.org/web/20141125150940im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c7046aac970b-120wi "Logo")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b7c7046aac970b-pi)It's the last few days to apply to the [Knight Cities Challenge](http://knightfoundation.us5.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=44a06e103b99cf80e07a2eaad&id=8eec36c4bf&e=bb27c06725), which closes for applications Friday Nov. 14 at 5 p.m. ET. The challenge seeks ideas that address the question: What’s your best idea to make cities more successful? The [Knight Foundation](http://knightfoundation.org/) is offering a share of $5 million for innovative ideas.

If you have specific questions, Knight will be holding a final virtual office hours session on Nov. 13, 3:00-4:00 p.m. ET. Please [RSVP here](http://knightfoundation.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=44a06e103b99cf80e07a2eaad&id=94c29e802d&e=bb27c06725).

To enter the Knight Cities Challenge and for more information, go to [KnightCities.org](http://knightfoundation.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=44a06e103b99cf80e07a2eaad&id=a233841d81&e=bb27c06725) and answer two brief questions by Nov. 14 (If you make it to the next level, there will be more required). Follow [#KnightCities](http://knightfoundation.us5.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=44a06e103b99cf80e07a2eaad&id=d434efcc4d&e=bb27c06725) and [@knightfdn](http://knightfoundation.us5.list-manage1.com/track/click?u=44a06e103b99cf80e07a2eaad&id=25d50c2797&e=bb27c06725) on Twitter for updates and please spread the word through your networks.
