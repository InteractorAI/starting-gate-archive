# Hispanic Unity, Broward SCORE team up to offer free entrepreneur workshop series in Spanish

Hispanic Unity is expanding its Emerging Entrepreneur Program by offering a series of free business workshops in Spanish with Broward SCORE.

The workshops aim to assist startups and established Hispanic business owners in South Florida, a rapidly increasingly business sector, by providing hands-on business tools. All the workshops are the Spanish-language versions of existing Broward SCORE seminars   led by bilingual business professionals. The workshops begin on July and continue throughout the fall.

(Quickbooks in Spanish, July 12)

(Starting a Business in Florida, July 19)
