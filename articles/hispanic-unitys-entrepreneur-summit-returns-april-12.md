# Hispanic Unity's Entrepreneur Summit returns April 12

[![2013 ESummit Logo landscape](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2017c381e78e4970b-250wi.jpg "2013 ESummit Logo landscape")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017c381e78e4970b-pi)Returning for a third
year,the[Entrepreneur Summit 2013](http://www.hufesummit.org/) is a day-long learning and
networking event which showcases the diverse array of businesses that
contribute to South Florida’s economy. This year’s event will beheld onFriday, April 12 at The
Westin Diplomat Resort & Spa in Hollywood from 7:30 a.m. – 2:30 p.m.

Produced
by Hispanic Unity of Florida,the
Entrepreneur Summit will provide emerging entrepreneurs and established
business owners with access to seasoned experts and educational resources to
help their businesses grow. Attendees are a mix of business-owners,
entrepreneurs, Fortune 500 companies, economic development specialists,
government staffers, small business experts, procurement officers, educational
institutions, media and corporate partners and others interested in
entrepreneurship.

The
event Keynote speaker is Keith Koenig, Founder and President of City Furniture, a retail
furniture company of 24 showrooms in South Florida under
two brands, City Furniture and Ashley Furniture HomeStores. There are workshops on angel investment, networking, marketing, Import/export, social entrepreneurship and more. [See the list of workshops and speakers here.](http://www.hufesummit.org/workshops.html)

Cost
for the entire event, including workshops, networking, Keynote Speaker Lunch
and the American Dreamer Awards Breakfast is $45 in advance, day-of
registration costs $55.  For more information visit [www.hufesummit.org](http://www.hufesummit.org/) or contactWendy DeLucca at [wdelucca@hispanicunity.org](https://web.archive.org/web/20130328183744/mailto:wdelucca@hispanicunity.org) or **[954-646-5403](https://web.archive.org/web/20130328183744/tel:954-646-5403).**
