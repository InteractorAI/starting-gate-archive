# How about a label for Miami Tech? Oui, Oui, Oui

[![UnnamedN2HWD3X3](https://web.archive.org/web/20141122211107im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d093874d970c-320wi "UnnamedN2HWD3X3")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d093874d970c-pi)

France wants to spotlight its high-growth-potential startups and to do that the French government has announced the creation of a new “French Tech" label.  “The aim is to do our utmost so that the Googles of the future are created and develop here in France,” summed up Fleur Pellerin, Minister of Culture and Communication, who launched the French Tech Initiative last autumn. With its new label, the government is also going to seek to spotlight cities that encourage high-growth-potential startups to develop in their territories.
