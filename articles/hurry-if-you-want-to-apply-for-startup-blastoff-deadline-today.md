# Hurry if you want to apply for SuperConf's Startup Blastoff -- deadline today

[![Superconflogo](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2017ee86945f4970d-120wi.jpg "Superconflogo")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017ee86945f4970d-pi)This is the last day to apply to pitch in the Startup Blastoff at [SuperConf](http://superconf.co/) on Feb. 21.

SuperConf's Startup Blastoff puts you in front of investor judges and you also compete for prizes worth at least $25K. Eight emerging tech companies will be selected for the opportunity, say Auston Bunsen and Brian Breslin, organizers of the event. The judges, who include investors and founders, will evaluate everything from the pitch to the technological achievements to the market viability of the company to determine a winner.

Find the link to apply on [Superconf.co](http://www.superconf.co/) and good luck! The eight companies selected will be announced later this week.
