# Entrepreneurial spaces joining the downtown Miami scene

**Shared workspaces in Miami’s downtown and Brickell areas are plentiful [(see the Miami Downtown Development Authority's list here)](http://miamidda.com/pdf/Downtown%20Co-Working%20Spaces%2007-14-14.pdf), but that’s not stopping two more focused on entrepreneurs from joining soon:**

\* **Mindwarehouse:** Set to open in September, mindwarehouse on the 7th floor at 111 NE 1st S. plans to curate its membership to offer expertise in the major components needed for building a successful business, including marketing, technology, legal, finance, mentorship, etc., said Brian Scheinblum, its founder and an entrepreneur in the hospitality industry. The space, which is pursuing a “green” certification for its interior, is 7,800 square feet to start with room to double, he said. More information: [mindwarehouse.com](http://www.mindwarehouse.com/)

\* **Building:** Built by tech entrepreneurs for tech entrepreneurs and in the planning stages for nearly two years, Building is being developed by the founders of Miami-based .CO Internet and STRAAT Investments. The three-floor, 16,000-square-foot co-working community center in West Brickell, complete with a roof garden, will cater to technology startups as well as growth-stage companies, said Jose Rasco of STRAAT. Building is expected to open by year’s end. More information: [Building.co](http://www.building.co/)
