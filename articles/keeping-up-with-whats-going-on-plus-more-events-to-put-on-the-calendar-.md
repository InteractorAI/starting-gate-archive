# Keeping up with what's going on: MiamiTech.org

For everyone wanting to keep up with the news of local startup scene, there's a new resource in town. [MiamiTech.org](http://www.miamitech.org/) has added an aggregation feature to its site -- click on News and you get posts by yours truly as well as [Startropica](http://www.startropica.com/), [Geeky Beach](http://www.geekybeach.com/) and others. [David Notik](http://www.linkedin.com/in/davidnotik) of [Woven](http://www.woven.org/) created the site, which also contains a user-friendly listing of tech and entrepreneurial events that is curated by Ed Toro. The MiamiTech.org team also asks for suggestions on making all aspects of the site more useful, and Notik says he has already received some great feedback. [Check it out.](http://www.miamitech.org/)
