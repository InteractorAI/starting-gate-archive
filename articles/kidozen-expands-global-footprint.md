# KidoZen expands global  footprint

[KidoZen](www.kidozen.com), an enterprise mobile app platform company based in Miami, said it has added eight new channel partners including large system integrators and enterprise mobility specialty firms across four continents. 4Mobi, Aptera, Apptivation, Brillio, Calance, Innovecs, NationSky and YellowLink  will address growing demand for KidoZen's platform in emerging markets such as Africa, Brazil, China, India and Mexico as well as Europe and the United States, KidoZen said in a press release.

 KidoZen said its channel partners, which now number 25 firms across 50 countries, deliver expertise in implementing end-to-end mobility solutions and services to help customers streamline business processes, boost employee productivity and drive innovation. In return, KidoZen gives partners access to technical, sales and marketing benefits to grow their business.

"By expanding our global partner footprint, we are able to satisfy market needs while delivering a fast, affordable and secure way of deploying enterprise mobile apps for our rapidly expanding customer base," said Jesus Rodriguez, founder and CEO of KidoZen.

 The KidoZen platform is used by organizations such as American Airlines, Boeing, DuPont, Pfizer, Microsoft, Shell, Toyota and United Healthcare.
