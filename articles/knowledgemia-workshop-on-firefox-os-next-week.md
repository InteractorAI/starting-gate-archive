# KnowledgeMIA workshop on Firefox OS announced for next week

The KnowledgeMIA is holding its second workshop
in its series: A member from Mozilla's Firefox Biz Dev team will share  plans for their new mobile operating system called
Firefox OS.

Given the power that Android (Google) and iOS
(Apple) have on the market, there is a need for a more open and flexible
alternative to continue to foster competition in the space, says Demian
Bellumio, who organizes KnowledgeMIA. “Mozilla will use their thousands of
developers to try to level the playing field in the mobile OS market, much in
the same manner as how they helped the browser market with their widely used
Firefox browser.,” he said, adding that this workshop is perfect for everyone
interested in the mobile space and in what impact this could have on their  businesses.

This workshop will be
free and will take place Thursday, Aug. 29, from 4 p.m. to 5:30 p.m. at LAB Miami. Register at <http://knowledgemiafirefox.eventbrite.com/> and
note that space is limited.
