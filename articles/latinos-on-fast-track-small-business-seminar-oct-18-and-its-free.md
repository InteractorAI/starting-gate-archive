# Latinos on Fast Track Small Business Seminar Oct. 18 -- and it's free

The Hispanic Heritage Foundation  and Verizon Wireless have joined forces to host the Latinos On Fast Track (LOFT) Small Business Seminar, a free event providing Latino entrepreneurs in South Florida with practical advice from top local entrepreneurs and business experts.

The LOFT seminar will be held Oct, 18, from 5 to 7:30 p.m. at the Hilton Downtown Miami, 1600 Biscayne Boulevard.

The event is designed to highlight industry-leading best practices, challenges and opportunities to boost growth and profitability through technology, innovation and other processes. It will feature interactive sessions and panel discussions with thought leaders and networking opportunities for South Florida small business owners.

Scheduled speakers and panel leaders include J. Antonio Tijorino, president of the Hispanic Heritage Foundation; Marisa Rivera, president of Mpowerment Works; Hessy Fernandez of Principal Strategy Group; and various civic and business leaders.

More information:  [www.hispanicheritage.org](http://www.hispanicheritage.org/).
