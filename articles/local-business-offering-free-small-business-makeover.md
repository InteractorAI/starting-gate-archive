# Local business offering free small business makeover

Just in time for the holiday season, [**Nexxus Management**](http://www.nexxusmanagement.com/) is offering a free Nexxus Holiday Small Business Makeover.  Nexxus, a provider of marketing, sales and Internet services to small and medium-sized businesses, is giving away one business makeover to a small business, said Rebecca Thomas, chief marketing officer for Nexxus.

Email [marketing@NexxusManagement.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20121205060511/mailto:marketing@NexxusManagement.com) and describe in 100 words or less why your business needs a makeover. Deadline: noon, Dec. 10. The company said the winner will get a consultation with the Nexxus Management team; a marketing analysis and next steps; and promotion on the Nexxus social media networks: [Facebook](http://www.facebook.com/NexxusManagement), LinkedIn and Twitter.
