# Making a Super (Bowl) splash

[![Dotcologo](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2017c364be18f970b-120wi.jpg "Dotcologo")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017c364be18f970b-pi)Whether or not you watch the big game, you’ve got to watch the commercials, right? This year be sure to watch the .CO  brand star in a [Super Bowl](http://www.nfl.com/superbowl/47) commercial with Go Daddy.  It’s the third year of Super placement for Miami’s own [.CO Internet](http://www.go.co/).

[Get a sneak peek of the "Your Big Idea" commercial here](http://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_detailpage&v=aE6ugHoIB_Q).

**Update:** On Tuesday, .CO launched [YourBigIdea.co](http://www.yourbigidea.co/), a fun website tied to the ad, also created by the Deutsch NY team. At YourBigIdea.co, users can type in their big idea, get suggested .CO domain names and see a customized glimpse of their fabulous future.

For more on .CO Internet, read [my recent story](http://www.miamiherald.com/2013/01/13/3179294/co-sets-sights-on-changing-the.html) and accompanying [Starting Gate post.](the-road-to-co.md)
