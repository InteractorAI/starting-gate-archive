# Medicast chosen for mobile health-tech accelerator

[Medicast](http://www.medicast.co/), the local company that delivers doctors on demand and launching first in South Florida, is one of 10 mobile health-related startup teams from across the country and Australia chosen for the inaugural [Sprint Mobile Health Accelerator powered by Techstars](http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&url=http%3A%2F%2Fsprintaccelerator.com%2F&esheet=50822564&newsitemid=20140312005313&lan=en-US&anchor=Sprint+Mobile+Health+Accelerator+powered+by+Techstars&index=1&md5=2ccea4c9caf25b094ec2b02a23f5d525). The three-month intensive mentor-driven program, located in Kansas City, officially kicks off this week.

The Sprint Mobile Health Accelerator powered by Techstars will provide entrepreneurs in this field opportunities to hone their business strategies and structure and be mentored by leading technology experts and entrepreneurs from around the country. Each startup receives up to $120,000 in funding. The program culminates in June when the 10 companies will pitch to an audience of investors.
