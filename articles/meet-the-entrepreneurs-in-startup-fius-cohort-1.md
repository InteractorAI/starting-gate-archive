# Meet the entrepreneurs in StartUP FIU's Cohort 1

[StartUP FIU](http://startup.fiu.edu/) launched its first cohort of its 13-week Empower incubator Sept. 6. The cohort, selected from 160 applications, includes 20 entrepreneurs from 19 businesses or concepts in a variety of industries. It also includes social entrepreneurs. The free incubator program accepted entrepreneurs from the idea stage to revenue-producing companies; it includes community members as well as FIU students and alumni. **Read more about the program here:** [Multi-campus StartUP FIU gets ready for takeoff](http://www.miamiherald.com/news/business/article82149147.html)
