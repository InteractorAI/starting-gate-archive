# Miami Fashion Network to launch online platform of trade resources

Miami Fashion Network said that on Thursday it is launching its
first online platform of fashion trade resources, built to better service the
needs of business owners and emerging and established designers in South Florida. The website, founded by Jessica Rae
Anderson, will also act as a hub for local suppliers to promote their services
to the fashion industry.

[Miamifashionnetwork.com](http://miamifashionnetwork.com/) will highlight everything needed to build
a fashion business, from patent attorneys, manufacturers, pattern makers and
contractors, to showrooms, photographers, importers/exporters and more.

The platform includes a fashion directory, event calendar,
newsfeed and blog, which are accessible to anyone, as well as an industry
bulletin, sourcing list and designer galleries that are for members only. The
platform is available in English, Spanish and Portuguese. For more information,
visit miamifashionnetwork.com or call 800-621-5540.
