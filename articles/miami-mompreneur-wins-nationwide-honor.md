# Miami mompreneur wins nationwide honor

[![Natalia Ortiz Head Shot](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2017c32fecec6970b-200wi.jpg "Natalia Ortiz Head Shot")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017c32fecec6970b-pi)A South
Florida mompreneur was one of 11 small business women nationwide honored as
part of the
 [Huggies MomInspired Grant Program](https://huggiesmominspired.com/).
Natalia Ortiz of Miami, pictured at right, and the other recipients each received $15,000 to help fund
their businesses.

Ortiz
founded [Two Hippos LLC](http://www.wallbumpi.com/), a small
business dedicated to inventing and manufacturing functional, practical and
stylish products for kids. Her company’s products include the Wall Bumpi bed
rail bumpers, the Cot Buddy cot mat, and the Monster Buddy body pillows for
kids.  The Wall Bumpi is an original safety device to help parents transition their child from a crib to bed safely. It provides a
cozier, safer sleeping solution for parents.
