# Milestone to celebrate: Guide app for iPad hits No. 1

[![IMG_0072](https://web.archive.org/web/20130712160144im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e20192abdb299b970d-300wi "IMG_0072")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e20192abdb299b970d-pi)With no fireworks or fanfare -–
not even a press release -– the [Guide iPad app](http://gui.de/ipad-app-download) was released on Monday. By
Wednesday afternoon it had hit No. 1 in the U.S. in the news category, says founder and CEO
Freddie Laker.

Led by Laker and COO Leslie
Bradshaw, Miami-based [Guide](http://gui.de/) turns your favorite online news, blogs and social
streams into video, using cutting edge avatar and text-to-speech technologies. The first release of the app contains more than 800 content
sources and offers a choice of seven virtual news anchors. “We'll be announcing
some very special and exciting content and character licensing deals in the
near future, too,” Laker said in a short note to supporters.

[![IMG_0068](https://web.archive.org/web/20130712160144im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201910412008f970c-300wi "IMG_0068")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201910412008f970c-pi)On
Wednesday afternoon, the app was also hovering around 80th  overall in the huge free category. Visit this link to download the app: <http://gui.de/ipad-app-download>

Laker said the team is hard at work on its Android tablet, Google TV, and web
versions of Guide. “We will have lots of new releases over the next three months to
make sure you can experience Guide where ever and whenever you want.”
