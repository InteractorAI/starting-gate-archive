# Mobile Payments Conference announces speaker lineup

*Mobile Marketing & Technology* today released the lineup of keynote speakers for the upcoming [Mobile Payments Conference](http://www.mobilepaymentconference.com/) to be held January 29-30, 2014, at the Miami BeachConvention Center in Miami, Florida.

 Brian Hamilton, SVP, National Small Business Capital One; Amol Deshmukh, Gemalto VP of mobile financial services North America; Don Bush, VP of marketing, Kount; Adam Granoff, VP of U.S. Solution Sales Team, MasterCard; William Clark President & CEO, Spindle, Inc.; Siva Narendra, CEO and co-founder, Tyfone; and Dean Siefert, SVP product strategy, Vantiv, will each deliver keynote presentations addressing the industry’s most pressing issues. To access the full agenda and list of speakers, please visit: <http://mobilepaymentconference.com/speakers>.

“The Mobile Payment industry is growing exponentially, with predictions of the US Market surpassing $1 Billion in 2013,” said Marla Ellerman, executive director and publisher of *Mobile Marketing & Technology,* in a press release. The Mobile Payments Conference will draw senior executives from all parts of the mobile wallet and mobile payments ecosystem, each of who will benefit from insight from our collection of experts confirmed as speakers. Attendees will be presented with firsthand commentary on trends and other factors affecting mobile commerce, with an emphasis on retail, online and person-to-person payments and strategies.
