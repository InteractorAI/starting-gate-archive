# More fund-raising news: Aeropost, Kitchen22 close financing deals

It’s venture capital week in South Florida.

In addition to previously reported rounds raised by cross-border e-commerce startup [iguama](miami-e-commerce-startup-iguama-scores-5-million-in-funding.md) of Miami, human resources software startup [Candidate.Guru](candidateguru-of-boca-attracts-950k-from-florida-angels-to-support-its-growtb.md) of Boca Raton, Miami tech startup [Tesser Health](tesser-health-raises-470000-in-funding-to-lower-prescription-drug-costs.md) and ed-tech startup [ClassWallet](new-classwallet-investment-follows-11-million-raised-in-q4.md) of Miami (scroll down to read about them), two more companies received funding, according to CB Insights’ daily report.

[Aeropost International Services](http://aeropost.com/), a Miami-based company in the mail and package forwarding business, closed a $4 million financing round from North Bridge Venture Partners. Its Miami hub in conjunction with partners throughout Latin America and the Caribbean allows the company to provide end-to-end service for the members of its network to receive their packages, internet purchases, mail and logistics services, CB Insights’ report said.

Food business [Kitchen22](http://kitchen22.com/)**,**founded by foodies and also of Miami, raised $800,000 from undisclosed investors. Kitchen22 wants to revitalize the consumer's experience by creating premium and innovative food products, while staying true to pure and simple ingredients.
