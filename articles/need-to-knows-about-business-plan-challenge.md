# Need to knows about Business Plan Challenge

**![Chair cropped](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201a3fcd5c252970b-320wi.jpg "Chair cropped") Business Plan Challenge at a glance**

**What to enter:**Three-page business plan

**Who can enter:** Entrepreneurs with businesses less than 2 years old or with under two years old or business ideas. There is a Community Track, FIU Track and High School Track.

**Entry deadline:** April 5

**Email entries to:** Challenge@MiamiHerald.com, FIUchallenge@MiamiHerald.com, highschoolchallenge@MiamiHerald.com

**Contest rules, judges’ bios, other info:** [MiamiHerald.com/challenge](http://www.miamiherald.com/challenge)

**Find out more:** E-mail Business Plan Challenge Challenge Coordinator Nancy Dahlberg at ndahlberg@miamiherald.com.
