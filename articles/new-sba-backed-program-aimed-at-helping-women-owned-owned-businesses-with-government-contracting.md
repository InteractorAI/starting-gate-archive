# New SBA-backed program aimed at helping women-owned businesses with government contracting

Did you know women-owned businesses are one of
the fastest-growing segments of new business in our economy? To help support
their growth, the Small Business Administration (SBA) along with American
Express OPEN and Women Impacting Public Policy (WIPP) announced the launch of **ChallengeHER** – a new national initiative aimed at
boosting government contracting opportunities for women-owned small businesses.

ChallengeHER
will offer women business owners the following to help them successfully
complete in the government contracting marketplace:

**\* Free events and workshops** across the country – in 2013, there are nine events
planned starting on May 23 with the Department of Energy;  
**\* Online curriculum and resources** that address various government contracting topics;  
**\* Mentoring opportunities** with experienced women contractors;  
**\* Access to government buyers** and prime contractors

can read more about the program [here](http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20130423005104/en).
