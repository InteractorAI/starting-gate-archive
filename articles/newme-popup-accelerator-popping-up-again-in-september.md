# NewME PopUp Accelerator popping up again in September

The fall season of the startup is opening strong with several events. We already told you about [Battle Hack Miami on Aug. 24 and 25](developers-battle-hack-miami-needs-you.md) to close out the summer. Here’s another event to put on your radar.

[NewME Accelerator](http://www.newmeaccelerator.com/) in San Francisco is bringing back its 3-day PopUp to Miami Sept. 27-29. This year it will again be hosted by The LAB Miami in Wynwood.

Like last year, the PopUp includes two  Master Classes in the evenings on preparing a pitch deck and presenting it like a pro. Also included is a one-on-one hour-long mentoring session and a Demo Night, where participants can present in front of local investors as well as ones from Silicon Valley and compete for prizes valued at more than $45,000, organizers say. One of the prizes is a spot in NewME’s Accelerator in San Francisco. The PopUp program fee is $99.

To find out more or to register, go here: <https://newme.rezdy.com/7509/newme-miami-popup>
