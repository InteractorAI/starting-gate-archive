# News: Flomio blows past its $80K Kickstarter goal

[![Flomio](https://web.archive.org/web/20121129051629im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017c340c6fab970b-320wi "Flomio")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017c340c6fab970b-pi)[Flomio](http://www.flomio.com/) surpassed its [$80K Kickstarter campaign goal](http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/flomio/flojack-nfc-for-ipad-and-iphone) -- with hours to spare -- to bring NFC technology to the Apple world. Powered by many donations big and small from the Miami startup community and others around the world, Flomio will now put the $96,145 raised into producing its product, the FloJack.

FloJack is a pocket-sized NFC (Near Field Communication) reader and writer that brings the power of NFC to the iPad, iPod Touch and the iPhone. Trggered by touch, NFC is a wireless technology that connects your phone to the devices around it in order to trigger new experiences. Until FloJack, it hasn't been available for iPhone, Flomio says.
