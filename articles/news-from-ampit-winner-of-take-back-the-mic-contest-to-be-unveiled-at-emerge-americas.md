# News from amp.it: Winner of Take Back the Mic contest to be unveiled at eMerge Americas

[![Brasil](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d1003c26970c-800wi.jpg "Brasil")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d1003c26970c-pi)

[![Colombia](https://web.archive.org/web/20150926140412im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d1003c49970c-800wi "Colombia")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d1003c49970c-pi)

[![Jamaica](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201bb081a9187970d-800wi.jpg "Jamaica")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201bb081a9187970d-pi)

Today, Miami-based tech startup [amp.it](http://amp.it/), founded by musician, entrepreneur and broadcast personality Derrick N. Ashong (DNA), announced the finalists for Season 1 of its first media property – [TAKE BACK THE MIC: THE WORLD CUP OF HIP HOP.](http://www.takebackthemic.com/)  They are ComboIO from Brazil, Crew Peligrosos from Colombia and Italee from Jamaica – all chosen from the competition’s inaugural country.sies by fans on [amp.it](http://www.amp.it/), a social network that amplifies the voices of a new generation, by rewarding fans for sharing the music they love.

Each four-minute, digital episode of [Take Back the Mic](http://www.takebackthemic.com/) gives the audience an artist’s-eye view of the streets of Rio de Janeiro, Medellín, and Kingston, as the artists struggle to overcome the odds, using their talent as their launching pad.  Of the 9 semifinalists championed by fans on [amp.it](http://amp.it/), 3 have advanced to the live May 4th finale to be held at Miami’s New World Center, where they will be joined by the highest-scoring fans. This culminating event will serve as the main entertainment for the [eMerge Americas](http://www.emergeamericas.org/) tech conference, launched last year by Medina Capital Founder and Managing Partner Manny Medina.

During the eMerge Americas party at the New World Center, fans will be able to vote at home for the winner of the World Cup of Hip Hop.

You can see the artist stories at [www.takebackthemic.com](http://www.takebackthemic.com/).
