# News: Venture Architects creating an online investor network

Venture Architects, a Miami business planning and investor preparedness firm for early and growth stage companies, is creating a new, online network to match investors and entrepreneurs.

[![MelissaKrinzmanNEW_bw](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2017d4111befd970c-200wi.jpg "MelissaKrinzmanNEW_bw")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017d4111befd970c-pi)The firm is inviting accredited investors nationwide — with a focus on Miami and New York — to view deals that match their investment criteria in such categories as deal stage, deal size and industry, said Melissa Krinzman, founder and managing director of Venture Architects.

Venture Architects Investment Network will not be a committed fund, but “a curated collection of smart, experienced and active investors, who will have the opportunity to view deals that they may otherwise never see for the purpose of becoming an investor and/or advisor,” Krinzman said.

      Accredited investors may join at  <https://gust.com/angel-group/venture-architects/join>. Entrepreneurs can apply at  <https://gust.com/angel-group/venture-architects/apply>
