# Open English featured on Small Empires with Alexis Ohanian on The Verge

[![Openenglish](https://web.archive.org/web/20141117083115im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201bb07a57b94970d-300wi "Openenglish")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201bb07a57b94970d-pi)

[Open English](http://www.openenglish.com/) is known for its viral-worthy TV ads and its revolutionary way of teaching English to the Latin American, Brazilian and US Hispanic markets. Open English is featured today in the second season of Small Empires, hosted by Alexis Ohanian, co-founder of reddit.

The Small Empires crew visited the headquarters in Coconut Grove in October. They met with Andres Moreno and the rest of the leadership team to discuss the business of online learning, tech start-ups in Miami, and lessons learned as leaders in the rapidly changing landscape of business and technology today. In the second episode of season 2, Small Empires visits  the world of Open English. You can watch it [here](http://www.theverge.com/2014/11/4/7151873/small-empires-season-2-episode-1-open-english).
