# Save the date: FIU’s AVCC to be Dec. 11-12

Florida International University’s [Pino Global
Entrepreneurship Center](http://entrepreneurship.fiu.edu/) will host its fourth annual Americas Venture Capital
Conference (AVCC) on Dec. 11 and 12 in Miami. This year’s theme will be the hot
topic of “Healthcare Innovation and
Venture Capital.”

The conference will spotlight new ventures and opportunities
in the healthcare sector, from products and processes to service delivery and
new business models, said Jerry Haar, FIU associate dean and director of the
Pino Center, housed at the College of Business. As in other years, two dozen
companies will be selected to present at the conference.

Calling Miguel B. ‘Mike’ Fernandez a visionary in the world of healthcare, Haar also announced that Fernandez, the chairman of [MBF Healthcare
Partners](http://www.mbfhealthcarepartners.com/), will co-chair the 2013 conference. MBF Healthcare Partners is a Coral
Gables-based private equity firm with committed capital in excess of $500
million that focuses on healthcare service companies nationwide.

The conference will include keynote speeches and interactive
sessions on mobile health, big data, health supply chain management and other topics,
a “shark tank” and plenty of time for networking and deal-making, Haar said.
