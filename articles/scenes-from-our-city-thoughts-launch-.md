# Scenes from Our City Thoughts launch

[![Oct2DSC_0234](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2019b025119e5970d-800wi.jpg "Oct2DSC_0234")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019b025119e5970d-pi)

[Our City Thoughts](http://www.ourcitythoughts.org/) launched with a mix of introspection and inspiration Friday at The LAB Miiami's new Idea Garden. Those attending participated in a discussion about Miami's future challenges and opportunities with a panel, recorded their own thoughts in the Cube, and got a video preview of what's to come on ourchitythoughts.org. Photos: Above: Our City Thoughts founder Binsen Gonzalez in front of the Cube painted by Davel Designs. Below, clockwise from left, the crowd participates in a Our City Thoughts Launch panel discussion including (pictured) Felecia Hatcher, Jose Hernandez-Solaun, Jordan Melnick, Jaret Davis and Frankie Coletto.  And Davel, the artist. Photos by Steve Viti.

The site will be a mix of video interviews, Q&As and spotlights on people who are "redefining Miami," says Binsen. You can see the new site here: [ourcitythoughts.org](http://www.ourcitythoughts.org/)
