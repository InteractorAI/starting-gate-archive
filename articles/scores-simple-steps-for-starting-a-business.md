# SCORE’s simple steps for starting a business

SCORE has developed
a 5-part workshop series that will take you through the basics of starting up a
business. Local SCORE chapters are running their series now. The agendas vary
slightly. Here’s SCORE Miami-Dade’s agenda:

1) Start-up Basics. A no nonsense look
at the requirements of starting a Business. The first free seminar has passed, but SCORE
will provide a makeup session at no charge if you sign up for the series.

The following workshops are to be taken as a group and cost $100
for all.

2) Business Concept Development: Thursday, Jan. 24, 5-7 p.m.

3) How to market your product or service: Thursday, Jan. 31, 5-7 p.m.

4) Creating Financial Forecasts and Reports: Thursday, Feb. 7, 5-7 p.m.

5) Funding Your Business: Thursday, Feb. 21, 5-7 p.m.

All workshops
are from 5 p.m. to 7 p.m. at: Shake-a-Leg Foundation, 2620 South Bayshore
Drive, Coconut Grove 33133.
