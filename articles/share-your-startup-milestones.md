# Share your startup milestones

Has your startup hit a milestone? Perhaps you've launched, completed a funding round, secured a big client, added a new product,  or were honored in some way.  We'd like to know about it and share your news. Please email me at [ndahllberg@miamiherald.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20121019030213/mailto:ndahllberg@miamiherald.com) and put startup milestone in the subject line.

And please do keep me posted on your developments, big and small. I can't promise I can report everything, but I'm interested in your story ideas and keeping up with the trends. Also, Starting Gate is always looking for guest posts. If you have an idea for a guest post or just to stay in touch, email me or tweet me @ndahlberg.
