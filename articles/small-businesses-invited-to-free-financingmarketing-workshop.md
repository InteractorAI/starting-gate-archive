# Small businesses invited to free financing/marketing workshop

Local small business owners and entrepreneurs can participate in
a free marketing and financing workshop in conjunction with the New York Stock
Exchange from 6 p.m. to 8 p.m. on Thursday, Oct. 11, at La Licuadora Restaurant
, 47 NE 2nd Ave. in Miami.

The “NYSE Big StartUp” is a national jobs-growth initiative that
connects young companies and entrepreneurs directly with corporate America, by
providing small companies with the resources they need to accelerate growth.

The “Small Business Connections: Marketing Meets Micro-financing”
workshop wiil be presented by NYSE in partnership with Accion, a national
microlender, and the online review site Yelp. Yelp Business outreach staff and
Accion loan consultants will be on hand to offer one-on-one consultations and
field questions from attendees. Specifics about the two key resources essential
for developing and growing any business in South Florida will be shared at the
workshop: knowledge of social media marketing and access to capital.

To register, visit: <http://yelpmiami.eventbrite.com/>
