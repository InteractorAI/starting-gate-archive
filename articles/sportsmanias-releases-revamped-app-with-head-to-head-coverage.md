# SportsManias releases revamped app with 'head-to-head' coverage

[SportsManias](http://www.sportsmanias.com/), the diehard sports fan’s source for team news, announced today the release of the new version of its mobile app, featuring “Head-to-Head” coverage for over 12,000 games a year with real-time updates from opposing teams’ professional beat writers.  “This will be the first news source where rivalries between professional beat writers is part of the game experience,” said Vicente Fernandez, co-founder of SportsManias. The redesigned app also now features a robust ‘rumors’ feed with push notifications for trade, free agency, and draft rumors on a fan’s favorite teams.

Today, over 1.6 million users utilize SportsManias to follow more than 700 teams and 22,000 tweets per day coming from over 3,000 beat writers who cover college, professional and international sports. The updated SportsManias app can be downloaded for free for iOS in the [iTunes Store](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/sportsmanias-sports-news-custom/id588722840?mt=8) and for Android devices in [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.antediem.sportsmanias).
