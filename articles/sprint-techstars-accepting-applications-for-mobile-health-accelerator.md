# Sprint-Techstars accepting applications for mobile-health accelerator

Mobile health-related startups ready to rev up their businesses are invited to [apply](https://www.f6s.com/sprintmobilehealthaccelerator2015#/apply)  to participate in the 2015 [Sprint Mobile Health Accelerator, Powered by Techstars](http://newsroom.sprint.com/presskits/sprint-accelerates-innovation.htm).  Ten startups will be selected.

The 2015 program will be conducted at the Sprint Accelerator facility in Kansas City March 9 through June 4, 2015. The 2014 Demo Day drew more than 1,500 attendees.

Participating companies relocate to Kansas City for the duration of the program. Each company receives up to $120,000 in funding. In 2015, Techstars is adding a first-of-its-kind [equity back guarantee](http://www.techstars.com/equity-back-guarantee/).
