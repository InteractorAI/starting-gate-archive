# Still time to enter eMerge Techweek Launch Competition

![Emerge+TW](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201a3fce26a99970b-300wi.jpg "Emerge+TW")Entrepreneurs will take center stage during the [eMerge Techweek Launch Competition](http://emergeamericas.org/experience/exhibitions/challenge), and South Florida startups still have a chance to enter.

Early- and later-stage companies will compete and the Top 100 applicants will receive space in eMerge's Startup Village. Twenty-five semi-finalists will present to a panel of judges for one chance to win cash and prizes. Early-stage winners will receive $50,000 and later-stage winners will receive $100,000.

But you can’t hit it out of the park, if you don’t step up to the plate. The application deadline for international companies to enter has passed, but local startups and later-stage companies can apply until Friday, April 4. Go here to find out more and register: <http://emergeamericas.org/experience/exhibitions/challenge>.
