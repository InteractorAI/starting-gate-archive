# SuperConf 2013: And the winners are...

The eight companies competing in [SuperConf](http://www.superconf.co/)'s Startup Blastoff were judged on everthing from the pitch to the technological achievements to the market viability of the company. Judges pelted the startups with questions about business models, their competition and the technology and offered healthy doses of feedback. Each of the eight companies was also offered 15 minutes with a local VC, organizer Auston Bunsen said.

SuperConf Blastoff winners are:

**

First Place, $22,000 in cash and prizes**

[Vinylfy](http://vinylfy.com/home), pitched by Jose Pimienta. Vinylfy helps record collectors to keep track of their collection, discover new exciting ones and share it with others online. Pimienta and his team launched last week. Judges liked the concept for this niche business, but were especially struck by his passion.

**Runner up, about $6,000 in cash and prizes**

[Boatsetter](http://www.boatsetter.com/userinvite/?next=/), pitched by Marc Billings, is the Airbnb of boating. Billings, who is partnering with Andrew Sturner of Hi-Lift Marina in Aventura on this startup, launched a private beta today.

The other companies that pitched on Thursday were:

[Feathr](http://www.linkedin.com/company/feathr); a multi-platform smartphone application that replaces paper business cards with interactive digital cards integrated with tools for contact management.  
[Gui.de](http://gui.de/): a software company focused on turning online news and blogs into video for anyone wanting to curate and consume content when reading isn’t an option.  
[Kimetric](http://www.kimetric.com/): integrated cloud based analytics, consumer profile and smart interactive displays for retail.  
[Pack Photos](http://getpack.co/):  an app that lets you create events and get your friends/attendees to contribute to a timeline of photos, comments and check-ins of the event in real time.  
[Seamless Docs](http://seamlessdocs.com/): transform any document so it can be completed online.  
[Tabber](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=br.com.unbound.tabber&hl=en): an app allowing you to order in your favorite restaurants and bars from your smartphone.

Congratulations to all the participants. Check back on Starting Gate for more on the winning company, the prizes and photos.

SuperConf continues Friday with nine speakers in the areas of design, development and entrepreneurship.
