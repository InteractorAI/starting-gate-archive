# Get involved in Startup Florida and take its survey -- link is below

***The following is from Susan Amat, co-founder and executive director of The Launch Pad at the University of Miami and tireless advocate for entrepreneurs and Startup Florida:***

Join the Startup Florida Team! Take the official Startup Florida survey for Startup America!  
  
 [![Amat_headshot](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201774438cf60970d-120wi.jpg "Amat_headshot")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201774438cf60970d-pi)Brian Breslin and I are among a group who have been leading the charge to coordinate efforts across the state for Startup Florida! On Monday, Startup America's Donna Harris lead a call to give our entrepreneurs throughout the state an overview of what the organization is all about and sharing best practices she and her team have learned over the last two years. We are ready to try new tactics- with new blood- to get Florida energized!  
  
Startup America has launched its initiative in 28 states and more to come. Connect to entrepreneurs in Florida and across the country but it all starts here at home. The slides from our call on Monday offer great background and a vision for tomorrow: <http://www.s.co/regions/startup-fl-deck> -- and you can visit this Startup America page to learn more about what the regions are all about: <http://www.s.co/regions/about>  
  
We have a survey that will help us gage your interests and needs -- and lets us know how you want to be involved in Startup Florida.
