# Tech Cocktail to debut Showcase & Mixer featuring 10 startups

[![Tech cocktail logo](https://web.archive.org/web/20140523050944im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a73dc779ff970d-250wi "Tech cocktail logo")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a73dc779ff970d-pi)

Tech Cocktail is debuting [Miami's Showcase & Mixer](http://tech.co/event/tech-cocktails-miami-mixer-startup-showcase-2) event June 3rd at The Stage, where 10 startups pitch while networking with other entrepreneurs and investors. Winners are chosen online and during the event, and they get a chance to pitch during [Tech Cocktail's Celebrate](http://celebrate.tech.co/) event in October. They also win bragging rights, says Tech Cocktail's Miami Community Manager Camila Sousa. Startups can sign up to pitch [here](http://tech.co/event/tech-cocktails-miami-mixer-startup-showcase-2)

"We will unveil the chosen 10 very soon," Camila says.
