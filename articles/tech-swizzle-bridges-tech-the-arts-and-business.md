# Tech Swizzle bridges tech, the arts and business

Tech Swizzle is a  new monthly networker with no agenda — and
that is by design.

**[![Techswizzle - Copy](https://web.archive.org/web/20130906070833im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019aff2bac9b970b-800wi "Techswizzle - Copy")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019aff2bac9b970b-pi)WHAT
AND WHY:** As our local entrepreneur
community grows, so should the opportunities to meet people who will make a
difference for your business. Tech Swizzle is more than networking, it is an opportunity to mix
business and pleasure.

Tech Swizzle is a Happy Hour networking event
bridging the tech, art, design, music and the business communities, which all fuel each other. Investors, programmers, developers,
designers, musicians, artists, entrepreneurs and service providers will have
the opportunity to freely mingle without an agenda over some cold beer and live entertainment.

Each Tech Swizzle event is only $5 per person
for unlimited drinks, or you can buy a yearly pass (good through December 2013)
for just $20 through our [Eventbrite page](https://techswizzle.eventbrite.com/).

Our kickoff event is
scheduled for Sept. 6 from 6-9pm with  live music from Miami band [Gold Dust Lounge](http://www.golddustlounge.com/).

**WHERE:** [LAB Miami](http://www.thelabmiami.com/)
in Wynwood, 400
NW 26th Street, Miami, FL 33127

**WHO:** Kristen McLean, Bookigee founder and CEO, pFunk Media and LAB Miami will organize this
monthly event, generously sponsored by Akerman Senterfitt law firm.
