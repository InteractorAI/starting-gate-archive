# TechCrunch Pitch-Off Miami: And the finalists are ...

[![Tcmeetups](https://web.archive.org/web/20170220155526im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d25fdd96970c-800wi "Tcmeetups")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201b8d25fdd96970c-pi)

The Miami [TechCrunch Meetup + Pitch-Off](https://techcrunch.com/event-info/meetup-pitch-off-miami-2017/) is Feb. 23, and the 10 competitors have just been announced. The startups will get 60 seconds to make their rapid-fire pitches to the audience and judges that will include local VCs and TechCrunch editors. The first place winner will receive a table for exhibiting at the upcoming TechCrunch Disrupt NY and runners-up will receive tickets to Disrupt.

Here are the finalists:

* [Apollonix](https://www.apollonix.com/)
* [ContextIsKey](https://www.contextiskeyapp.com/)
* [DocuVital](https://www.docuvital.com/)
* [Donkies Technology](http://www.donkies.co/)
* [DreamsCloud](https://www.dreamscloud.com/)
* [Emerge](https://emerge.me/)
* [Ezzy A](https://www.ezzya.com/)
* [R3ports, Inc](http://www.r3ports.com/)
* [RecordGram Inc](http://www.recordgram.com/)
* [Salesmsg](https://salesmsg.com/its-coming-soon)

Even if you’re not on these lists, you’ll still have a shot to hop on stage because TechCrunch will be  raffling off one spot in the pitch-off as the WILDCARD startup.

Attendee tickets are $10 and the event, from 6 p.m. to 10 p.m., will be at the Paris Theater in Miami Beach.

[You can buy tickets here.](https://techcrunch.com/event-info/meetup-pitch-off-miami-2017/)
