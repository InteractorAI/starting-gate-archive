# 18 South Florida startups presenting at ETBS on Friday

[![Etbs15presentingcos](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2017c33fe92dd970b-320wi.jpg "Etbs15presentingcos")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017c33fe92dd970b-pi)The 15th annual  [Emerging Technology Business Showcase](http://www.edc-tech.org/index.php?src=events&submenu=EDC_Events&srctype=detail&category=Annual%20Event&refno=132) is the [Enterprise Development Corporation](http://www.edc-tech.org/) of South Florida's  premier pitch event for emerging science and technology startups. Members of the entrepreneurial, tech and angel community convene on Friiday, Nov. 30 to hear the presentations and meet the teams of 18  promising young South Florida companies, hear from a panel of VCs and angels and participate in networking opportunities throughout the day. To learn more about the event, go [here](http://www.edc-tech.org/index.php?src=events&submenu=EDC_Events&srctype=detail&category=Annual%20Event&refno=132).

At right are the startups that will be presenting on Friday. Ea:h company will give a 12-minute pitch and field questions for 3 minutes.

Congratulations to the presenting companies.
