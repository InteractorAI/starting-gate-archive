# Still time to enter the Miami Herald Business Plan Challenge -- here's how!

[![Challenge_Chair](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2016763d5bac1970b-120wi.jpg "Challenge_Chair")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2016763d5bac1970b-pi)You still  have plenty of time to enter the 2012 [Miami Herald Business Plan Challenge](http://www.miamiherald.com/challenge), our annual entrepreneurship contest open to entrepreneurs with startup businesses two years old or less as well as entrepreneurs with business ideas. There are three tracks for entry: A community track, open to all South Floridians, an FIU track open to students and alumni of that university, and a high school track. Your three-page business plan is your entry, which must be in by March 24.

Melissa Krinzman and Darius Nevin, two Challenge [judges](http://www.miamiherald.com/2012/02/05/2623681/meet-the-judges-community-track.html), gave a great bootcamp last week. If you missed it, here's a [story](http://hrld.us/wXtYgC ) that will give you a recap of their top tips.

Need more incentive? Here are [five reasons](http://www.miamiherald.com/2012/03/11/2676607/5-reasons-you-should-enter-the.html) to enter the Business Plan Challenge.

Find the rules of the Challenger [here](http://www.miamiherald.com/2012/02/05/2624682/2012-business-plan-challenge-rules.html) and more information [here.](http://www.miamiherald.com/challenge)

Email your entries to [challenge@miamiherald.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20120322192546/mailto:challenge@miamiherald.com) (community track); [fiuchallenge@miamiherald.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20120322192546/mailto:fiuchallenge@miamiherald.com) (FIU track) and [highschoolchallenge@miamiherald.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20120322192546/mailto:highschoolchallenge@miamiherald.com) (high school track).
