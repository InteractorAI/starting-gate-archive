# This just in: IBM SmartCamp coming to Miami, seeking startups in healthcare

*This just in from IBM -- a great opportunity for South Florida's entrepreneurial community! Deadline to apply to present at IBM SmartCamp KickStart Miami is April 27:*

IBM is looking to team with healthcare technology startups to turn data into clinical and business insights for better quality of care.     
  
The global healthcare industry is experiencing fundamental transformation.  With increasing demands from consumers for enhanced healthcare quality and increased value, healthcare providers and payers are under pressure to deliver better outcomes. Do you have a [Smarter Healthcare](http://www.ibm.com/smarterplanet/us/en/healthcare_solutions/ideas/index.html?re=spf) solution?  Apply toay to present your company to a live audience and a chance to network with world-class venture capitalists, investors, angels and healthcare industry experts.  
 **SmartCamp KickStart Miami**is an exclusive event aimed at identifying early stage entrepreneurs who are developing healthcare business ventures that align with the IBM Smarter Planet vision. Selected participants will receive:   
·        Mentorship from world class investors and advisors   
·        Exposure to IBM Healthcare Executive Leadership and Business Development Leaders   
·        Potential advancement to IBM’s North American Regional SmartCamp  
  
If that sounds like what your start-up needs and your company is less that 5 years old, privately held and has a solution to fit this demand, [apply](http://www.f6s.com/ibmsmartcampkickstartmiami) by April 27 to participate in **SmartCamp KickStart Miami** to be held May 15, 2012.  Healthcare startups from anywhere in the world can register for this exclusive event.   
  
**SmartCamp**is a part of the **IBM Global Entrepreneur** initiative, which helps you build your business around the smarter planet market opportunity.  By leveraging the benefits of IBM Global Entrepreneur, you can gain access to no-charge IBM software, technical enablement resources, mentoring, greater credibility, and validation of your solution.   To join and learn more, visit [ibm.com/isv/startup](http://ibm.com/isv/startup).
