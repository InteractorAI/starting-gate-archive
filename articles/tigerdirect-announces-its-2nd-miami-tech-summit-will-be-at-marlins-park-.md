# TigerDirect announces its 2nd Miami Tech Summit will be at Marlins Park

> [![Techball-150x150](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2019aff963724970c-120wi.jpg "Techball-150x150")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2019aff963724970c-pi) TigerDirect.com announced
> plans today for its second annual [Miami Tech Summit and
> Holiday Bash](http://www.sfltechsummit.com/).  TigerDirect, a retailer specializing in consumer
> electronics, technology products and computer services, and Intel will host an evening showcasing the latest and
> greatest in  emerging technology and
> interactive entertainment at  Marlins
> Park on Nov. 7.
>
>  Capitalizing on the success
> of last year’s event, with over 4,000 attendees and special celebrity
> appearances by Anna Kendrick, Reggie Bush and Ryan Tannehill,
> TigerDirect and Intel chose to double down with Marlins Park.
> The Miami Tech Summit and Holiday Bash will allow vendors to announce new
> products and do pre-release demonstrations for an anticipated 10,000 attendees. The event
> will take full advantage of Marlins
> Park’s space, including its
> high-tech score boards and LED screens. The
> concourse surrounding the field will feature over 100 vendors, each showcasing
> new products and services.
>
> > Participating
> > exhibitors include Intel, Western Digital, Alienware, HP, Kingston, Microsoft,
> > Seagate, Patriot, SMS Audio, ASUS, Discount Laptop Shop, Centon, CoolerMaster,
> > Sony (VAIO), eBay, Tripplite, Epson, Thermaltake, TCL (TTE), Swann, Planar,
> > PayPal, Night Owl, LG, Belkin/Linksys, AT&T Wireless, United Stationaries,
> > NZXT / iBuyPower, Kensington, Autodesk, Squaretrade, Verbatim, XFX, Viewsonic,
> > TrendMicro, Sandisk, MSI, EVGA, OCZ, Drobo and Actiontech. The first 5,000
> > consumers and first 1,000 business customers will receive a complimentary “swag
> > bag” from TigerDirect.  All attendees
> > will be eligible to participate in various additional giveaways throughout the
> > night.
> >
> > The second annual Miami Tech Summit and Holiday Bash is free and  will take place on Thursday, Nov. 7, 2013 from 8pm – 12 midnight. This event will be open to the public and details will be here:  at[http://www.miamitechbash.com](http://www.miamitechbash.com/)
>
