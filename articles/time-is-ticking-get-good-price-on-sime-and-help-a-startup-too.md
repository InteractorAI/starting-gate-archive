# Get great price on Sime MIA and help a local startup, too

[![Logo](https://web.archive.org/web/20141109205957im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201bb07a73cdb970d-300wi "Logo")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201bb07a73cdb970d-pi)At Refresh Miami Thursday night, Demian Bellumio announced special pricing for Sime MIA – but the clock is ticking.

The “Bring Your Fans to Sime MIA” is a challenge sponsored by the [Knight Foundation](http://www.knightfoundation.org/)  with the support of [Refresh Miami](https://www.refreshmiami.com/), [LAB Miami](http://thelabmiami.com/), the [Miami Chamber of Commerce](https://www.miamichamberofcommerce.com/) and many other local partners.  The challenge  runs for just 48 hours and it started at 10 am on Nov. 6th.

The point is to help local startups be  highly visible at Sime MIA, therefore 15 of the best local startups were selected to compete for the chance to exhibit at The Summit at Sime on Dec. 2nd with their  own Demo Pod (worth $5K).

But they will need your help to win! Each startup is listed with a special code that its fans can use to purchase heavily discounted tickets (90 percent off) to The Summit @ Sime MIA.  The five startups that get the most fans to purchase tickets will win the demo pods.

The startups are: Videoo, Everypost, Flyinstyle, Flat Out of Heels, PropelU, FiNe, LiveNinja, Hello Show, Accredify, Glip, Blackdove, Wyncode, AdMobilize, Amp.It, YellowPepper.

Using your chosen startup’s discount code, you can get tickets for The Summit for just $48  during the 48-hour period by entering it as a promotional code.

It’s a win-win, said Bellumio. So go to [Simemia.co](http://www.simemia.co/) to order your ticket –  the clock is ticking.
