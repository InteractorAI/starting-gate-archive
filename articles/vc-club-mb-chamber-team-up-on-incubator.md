# VC Club, MB Chamber team up on incubator

This was submitted by Brandon Young of the [Micro Venture Capital Club](http://www.microvcclub.com/), which is partnering the the [Miami Beach Chamber](http://www.miamibeachchamber.com/) on an incubator and is seeking entrepreneurs and intestors. Here is his post:

The Micro Venture Capital Club and the Miami Beach Chamber of Commerce have joined forces to launch an Incubator Initiative here in South Florida.   The goal of this unique incubator will be to find entrepreneurs with brilliant ideas and help them take their idea to the next level by formalizing it into a professional business venture.  Entrepreneurs selected to participate in this effort will relocate to Miami Beach for a 2-3 month period, work directly with professional business consultants to create solid business plans and receive the necessary tools and resources to secure capital funding for their ventures.

The Micro VC Club and the Miami Beach Chamber of Commerce will be calling on its members, contacts, prestigious local based international business leaders, and local venture capitalists to participate in this premier incubator. “Ultimately, we want to find people who have great business ideas and are serious about making them a reality.  With this incubator, we want to foster innovation and what better place to do it than in beautiful Miami Beach” commented Micro VC Club President, Brandon M. Young.

Submissions to participate in the inaugural class of the Incubator Initiative are currently being accepted.  In addition, entrepreneurs have the opportunity to attend monthly Micro VC Club meetings and present their ideas to club members comprised of potential investors.

For inquiries on how you can participate or submit an idea for either the monthly club meetings or the Incubator Initiative, please visit [www.MicroVCclub.com](http://www.microvcclub.com/) or contact [BYoung@MicroVCclub.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20120319035245/mailto:BYoung@MicroVCclub.com)
