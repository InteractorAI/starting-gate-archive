# Weather Kitty hits the iPhone store

[![Outdoorsy_Partly_Miami (1)](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201901eb64c6a970b-300wi.jpg "Outdoorsy_Partly_Miami (1)")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201901eb64c6a970b-pi)Here’s a development
that may even make [Grumpy Cat](https://www.facebook.com/TheOfficialGrumpyCat) smile. [Weather Kitty](http://www.weatherpuppy.com/kitty/) has hit the iPhone store!

The most famous cat in
the world – with over 1 million Facebook fans – stars in a special themed weather
app for $1.99. But if you’d rather have your own cat bring you your weather
news, you can do that too. And like the popular [Weather Puppy](http://weatherpuppy.com/), created by a Miami
startup, the free app comes with many cute and cuddly cat images that are customized to
the time of day and weather conditions. Free themes include Outdoorsy Kitty and
Summer Fun; there’s also a Yoga Cats version for $1.99.

And there is more news
from [Shiv Takhar](https://twitter.com/ShivTakhar) and [Suraj Hemnani](https://twitter.com/SurajHemnani), the team that brought you Weather Puppy. The
team has recently added more than 400 more images to Weather Puppy, so there
are now more than 500 in the app. They have also added hourly weather forecasts and an additional two
days of future forecasts (now 5 days). The images in the app are now Retina
& iPhone 5 optimized. Weather Puppy alone has been downloaded more than
200,000 times and has hit the #1 spot for free weather apps in 19 countries,
including Spain, Mexico, United Kingdom, France and Germany.

Weather Puppy also has
two fund-raising themes in the app - Dogs Trust (the largest dog charity in the
UK) and Detroit Dog Rescue. “Detroit has the highest number of homeless dogs in
the country - some estimates are as high as 50,000. We are on pace to
raise about $10k for the charity in our first 12 months of having the
fund-raising themes,” Hemnani said.

But everyone knows the
Internet loves cats, and Weather Kitty came out of the gate very strong earlier
this week, already hitting the No. 2 showing to iPhone weather apps. Securing a
partnership with Grumpy Cat’s people took a while, but will be well worth it, Hemnani
said. The themes will be continually updated and more images will be added to
both the free and $1.99 offerings. Like Weather Puppy, the app is designed to be fun and make you smile.

Now that they’ve
satisfied the dog and cat people, what’s next? “You’ll have to stay tuned!”
Hemnani said.
