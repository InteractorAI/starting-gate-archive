# What do entrepreneurs need? Take this survey

Please fill out a survey from the Miami-Dade Small Business Development Center and University of Miami’s Launch Pad to help determine the needs of entrepreneurs in Miami-Dade County. Results of the survey will be used to help tailor SBDC’s support and resources to your needs, whether your small business is new or existing. Find the survey at: [www.thelaunchpad.org/sbdc](http://www.thelaunchpad.org/sbdc)
