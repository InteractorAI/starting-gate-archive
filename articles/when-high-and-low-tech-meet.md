# When high and low tech meet

Yesterday we blogged about [FIU's](www.fiu.edu "FIU") unique high-low-tech solution -- drone and the dog -- to seek out avocado disease.

Today at the Taiwan pavillion we spotted another intriguing technology clash from [Relax FIR Master](www.fir-master.com), where a technology  device (a lamp) projects traditional chi energy to heal, re-energize and improve circulation...a natural alternative, if you will, to Botox. We didn't have enough time to figure out if it would really tighten our skin (not to mention thighs) but it sure sounds good.
