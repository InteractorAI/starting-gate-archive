# Who's a changemaker? Still time to highlight those making a difference

[Ashoka South Florida's](http://usa.ashoka.org/ashoka-south-florida) social media campaign for July I told you  about is halfway over but there's still plenty of time to nominate a South Florida changemaker. Nearly 300 people have been highlighted so far from all industries and disciplines, organizers say. One goal is to shine a light on lesser-known changemakers and recognize them for their accomplishments. At the end there will be a meetup; details to come.

 Getting involved is easy. Use #30daysofchangemiami to highlight a changemaker on Twitter, and there is also a Facebook page -- [30 Days of Change Miami](https://www.facebook.com/groups/312414762247207/). #tagyoureit
