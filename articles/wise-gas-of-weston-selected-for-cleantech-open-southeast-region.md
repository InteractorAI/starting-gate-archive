# Wise Gas of Weston selected for Cleantech Open Southeast Region

[![Logocto_se_small](https://web.archive.org/web/20160820172613im_/http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201901d05e725970b-200wi "Logocto_se_small")](https://www.facebook.com/wisegas)
  
[Wise Gas](https://www.facebook.com/wisegas)  of Weston is one of 24 semifinalists
selected to participate in the  [Cleantech
Open Southeast Region](http://cleantechopen-southeast.org/), the regional level of the 2013 [Cleantech Open](http://www.cleantechopen.org/) national accelerator
program. The companies were selected from an elite group of cleantech
entrepreneurs that applied from across the Southeastern United States,
including Washington, D.C.,
Georgia, Florida,
Research Triangle
Park and Tennessee.  
  
Semifinalists
concentrate on innovation across the following technology categories
Agriculture, Water, & Waste; Chemicals & Advanced Materials; Energy
Distribution and Storage; Energy Efficiency; Energy Generation; Green Building;
and Information Technology & Communications.

[![LOGO546470_405798832782809_1490943852_a](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201901d05eb7c970b-200wi.jpg "LOGO546470_405798832782809_1490943852_a")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201901d05eb7c970b-pi)[Wise Gas](http://www.wisegasinc.com/), which won
in the Energy Generation category, sells CNG Equipment of all sizes, builds CNG
Stations, converts vehicles to run on CNG and offers professional consulting
services nationwide. Two other companies from Florida
were also selected: [SIGARCA](http://www.sigarca.com/Welcome.html),  a Gainesville company in the Agriculture- Water- Waste
category, and [Trash 2 Cash-Energy](http://www.trash2cashenergy.com/), a Tampa
company in the Energy Generation category.

Benefits for [the 24 regional finalists](http://cleantechopen-southeast.org/2013/06/cleantech-open-southeast-region-announces-24-business-accelerator-semi-finalists/) (e.g. those eligible to
compete for the national grand prize) include an award package that consists of
combined cash and in-kind services worth up to $20,000. The Southeast regional finalists
will join other regional winners from across the United
States to contend for the national title at the Cleantech
Open’s Global Investor Forum on November 20-22, 2013 in
San Jose, California
and a chance to secure the grand prize of up to $200,000 in investment and
services.
