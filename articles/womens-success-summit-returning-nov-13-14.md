# Women's Success Summit returning Nov. 13-14

More than 700 women are expected to attend the upcoming two-day [Women.s Success Summit VI](http://www.womenssuccesssummit.com/), “Work & Play Every Day,”  on Nov. 13 and 14 at Briza on the Bay, 1717 N. Bayshore Dr., Miami.

Touted as Miami’s largest business conference for women, the summit “is designed to jumpstart businesses, motivate and educate, all while connecting professionals with other high-powered, like-minded businesswomen.” The summit features workshops, panel discussions, one-on-one coaching, networking exercises, and live, interactive onstage activities designed to impart practical knowledge and applicable skills to help women successfully market themselves and rise above any competition – male or female.

[![Villalobos](../images/6a00d83451b26169e2017d3d41f495970c-120wi.jpg "Villalobos")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e2017d3d41f495970c-pi)The lineup of expert panelists and successful business owners includes: [Michelle Villalobos](2-miami-based-women-entrepreneurs-win-national-contest-.md) (pictured), founder and director of the Women’s Success Summit; Jessica Kizorek, founder of BadassBusinessWomen.org; MarlyQ, event producer and founder of 5K Freedom Run; Joe Calhoon, a speaker with Covey Leadership Center and author of *Prioritize!* and *On The Same Page*; Kathy Andersen, author of *Change Your Shoes, Live Your Greatest Life*; and Bruce Turkel, CEO  
of the brand management firm TURKEL.

The conference’s costs include Day 1 passes for $67 and VIP two-day passes for $147.

For more information and to purchase tickets, visit [www.WomensSuccessSummit.com](http://www.womenssuccesssummit.com/).
