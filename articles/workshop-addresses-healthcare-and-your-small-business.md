# Workshop addresses healthcare and your small business

Broward SCORE is offering a workshop on Tuesday about employee healthcare and how upcoming changes can impact your business, including:

* \* Who is covered, who is excluded
* \* Heathcare provisions changes already in place and provisions that are scheduled to happen
* \* Tax credits that will benefit small businesses with fewer than 10 employees who offer medical  
  coverage
* \* Mandates that will impact businesses or employees of businesses with more than of fewer than 50 employees
* \* Tax penalties that will be in place for employers and employees who do not offer or have medical coverage
* \* The benefits and challenges of healthcare to small businesses
* \* Guidelines for shopping for medical insurance

 The presenter is Matthew Stein.
