# Wyncode's first Pitch Day: And we have a winner ...

[![Wyncode2](../images/6a00d83451b26169e201a73de5e8f7970d-800wi.jpg "Wyncode2")](http://miamiherald.typepad.com/.a/6a00d83451b26169e201a73de5e8f7970d-pi)

[Wyncode's](http://www.wyncode.co/) first ever Pitch Day last night attracted more than 150 people to The LAB Miami. The 14 members of the first cohort of the coding school bootcamp learned programming skills – many starting at ground zero. In the last two weeks of the nine-week program, they created projects -- including [labcollective.woven.co](http://wyncode.cmail1.com/t/d-l-vtyuud-mdlziku-d/), [www.meme.menu](http://wyncode.cmail1.com/t/d-l-vtyuud-mdlziku-h/), [www.feelheartbeat.com](http://wyncode.cmail1.com/t/d-l-vtyuud-mdlziku-k/), [familyreunion.herokuapp.com](http://wyncode.cmail1.com/t/d-l-vtyuud-mdlziku-u/), [www.goodmeasure.io](http://wyncode.cmail1.com/t/d-l-vtyuud-mdlziku-b/) and  [www.wythme.co](http://wyncode.cmail1.com/t/d-l-vtyuud-mdlziku-n/) – which they presented to a team of friendly judges from Miami’s tech community, who offered feedback and suggestions. Matt Mawhinney, pictured below, won the Rokk3r Labs challenge, winning a three-month paid internship at Rokk3r paying $9,000, the cost of  his Wyncode tuition. CareCloud offered $5,000 signing bonuses to any Wyncode students it hires.  Wyncode’s next cohorts are July 21–Sept. 19 and Oct. 20-Dec. 19. Find out more at Wyncode.co.
